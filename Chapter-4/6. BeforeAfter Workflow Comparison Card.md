## 6. Before/After Workflow Comparison Card

Keep this visible until the layered method becomes second nature.

| Old One‑Shot Workflow | New Layered Workflow |
|------------------------|----------------------|
| One giant prompt with structure, tone, pacing, and voice mashed together. | Four sequential layers, each dedicated to one creative dimension. |
| 2+ hours editing a draft that’s structurally confused, tonally flat, and poorly paced. | 5–10 minutes per layer reviewing and refining before moving on. |
| Errors compound; a bad hook ruins pacing you only notice at the final read. | Errors caught at the layer where they originate and fixed in seconds. |
| Every script generation feels like a gamble. | Every script follows a repeatable manufacturing process. |
| Editing is a grueling rewrite tax. | Editing is a light polish after four validated layers. |
