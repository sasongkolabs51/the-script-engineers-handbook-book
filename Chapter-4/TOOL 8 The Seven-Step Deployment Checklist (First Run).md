## TOOL 8: The Seven-Step Deployment Checklist (First Run)

### *Follow this step by step for your first layered prompting session. Time yourself. You'll be shocked how fast it is.*

### Step 1: Prepare Workspace (2 min)

☐ Open a new document / note / conversation thread
☐ Title it: "Layered Prompting — [Video Title]"
☐ Copy the workspace template (Tool 1) or create four labeled sections
☐ Have your content calendar / topic idea ready
☐ Have your Voice DNA Profile (Tool 5) open for reference

---

### Step 2: Run Layer 1 — Ideation (5–8 min)

☐ Fill in the Layer 1 prompt template (Tool 2) with your specifics:
  - Topic: _______________________________________________
  - Angle: _______________________________________________
  - Audience: _______________________________________________
  - Emotional outcome: _______________________________________________
  - Structure: _______________________________________________
  - Personal element: _______________________________________________
☐ Execute the prompt
☐ Review the beat sheet against the Layer 1 Handoff Checklist (Tool 3)
☐ All 7 criteria pass? ☐ Yes → Save beat sheet, proceed. ☐ No → Refine and regenerate.

**Layer 1 complete time:** ___:___

---

### Step 3: Run Layer 2 — Structural Skeleton (8–12 min)

☐ Paste the approved beat sheet into the Layer 2 prompt template
☐ Add word count target: approximately _________ words
☐ Execute the prompt
☐ Read the skeleton in a monotone voice (literally, out loud, in a flat voice)
☐ Review against the Layer 2 Handoff Checklist (Tool 3)
☐ All 9 criteria pass? ☐ Yes → Save skeleton, proceed. ☐ No → Fix the specific failing beat, regenerate that section.

**Layer 2 complete time:** ___:___

---

### Step 4: Run Layer 3 — Pacing Injection (5–8 min)

☐ Paste the approved skeleton into the Layer 3 prompt template
☐ Add emotional stage directions per section (use Tool 4 templates as guide)
☐ Execute the prompt
☐ **Read the entire output ALOUD.** This is non-negotiable. Your ear catches what your eyes miss.
☐ Review against the Layer 3 Handoff Checklist (Tool 3)
☐ All 9 criteria pass? ☐ Yes → Save paced script, proceed. ☐ No → Adjust stage directions, regenerate.

**Layer 3 complete time:** ___:___

---

### Step 5: Run Layer 4 — Voice Overlay (5–8 min)

☐ Paste the approved paced script into the Layer 4 prompt template
☐ Paste your Voice DNA Profile (or the relevant sections)
☐ Execute the prompt
☐ **Read the final script ALOUD.** Does it sound like you talking?
☐ Review against the Layer 4 Handoff Checklist (Tool 3)
☐ All 9 criteria pass? ☐ Yes → Script is recording-ready. ☐ No → Use targeted revoice (Tool 6) for specific lines.

**Layer 4 complete time:** ___:___

---

### Step 6: Self-Assessment (3 min)

Answer these four questions honestly:

| Layer | Question | Answer |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | "Would I be excited to record this video based on the beat sheet alone?" | ☐ Yes ☐ No |
| 2 | "Does the skeleton make a compelling argument even without style?" | ☐ Yes ☐ No |
| 3 | "Does the aloud reading feel natural and emotionally varied?" | ☐ Yes ☐ No |
| 4 | "Would my most loyal subscribers recognize this script as mine?" | ☐ Yes ☐ No |

**Any "No" answers?** Go back to that layer. Fix it. Don't record a script you're not proud of. The whole point of this system is that you CAN fix it in minutes, not hours.

---

### Step 7: Final Check & Record (2 min)

☐ One final read-through of the complete script
☐ Mark any last word swaps or micro-adjustments (do them now, not in editing)
☐ Confirm all [B-ROLL] notes are clear enough for your editing session
☐ Save the completed workspace (Tool 1) for your records
☐ **Hit record.**
