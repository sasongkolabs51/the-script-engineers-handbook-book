## 3. Handoff Criterion Checklist (Print and Use During Every Run)

Use this as a gate before moving to the next layer. Don’t skip it—this is what keeps errors from compounding.

### After Layer 1 (Ideation)
- [ ] The angle is specific, not something anyone could say.  
- [ ] I can clearly state what the viewer should feel by the end.  
- [ ] The beats follow a logical, emotionally engaging order.  
- [ ] I’m genuinely excited about this video based on the beat sheet.

### After Layer 2 (Structural Skeleton)
- [ ] The argument holds together even when read without style.  
- [ ] The stakes are personal and clear.  
- [ ] Transitions between beats are smooth.  
- [ ] [HOOK] and [CTA] are in sensible spots.

### After Layer 3 (Pacing Injection)
- [ ] When I read aloud, the pauses land where they feel natural.  
- [ ] No section feels rushed when it should be reflective, or slow when it should be urgent.  
- [ ] B‑roll prompts describe what the viewer should see and are placed at logical visual breaks.

### After Layer 4 (Voice Overlay)
- [ ] This sounds like me—not a generic “conversational” tone.  
- [ ] A friend or loyal subscriber would recognize my voice.  
- [ ] All pacing tokens and structural beats are still intact.
