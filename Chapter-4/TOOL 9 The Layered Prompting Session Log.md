### The Progress Markers

You'll know the system is working when:

- ☐ Your total time drops below 60 minutes consistently (usually by session 4–5)
- ☐ You stop needing loop-backs after Layer 2 (your ideation prompts get sharper)
- ☐ Layer 4 output sounds like you on the first try (your Voice DNA Profile is dialed in)
- ☐ You no longer feel anxiety before starting a script (the process is predictable)
- ☐ Your editing time drops below 5 minutes per script (the rewrite tax is dead)
- ☐ You can produce a script on a topic you've never covered before, using the same process, and it still sounds like you
