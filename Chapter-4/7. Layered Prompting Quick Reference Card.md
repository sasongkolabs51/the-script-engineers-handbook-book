## 7. Layered Prompting Quick Reference Card

*Keep this one‑pager handy until the workflow becomes automatic.*

**The Four Layers (in order):**  
1. **Ideation** → Topic, angle, emotional outcome, beat sheet (bullet points, no prose).  
2. **Structural Skeleton** → Raw, bland prose; validate logic, transitions, stakes, [HOOK] and [CTA] placeholders.  
3. **Pacing Injection** → Add delivery tokens ([Pause 1s], [Fast energy], [B‑roll moment], etc.); read aloud.  
4. **Voice Overlay** → Apply your specific voice profile while preserving all pacing tokens and structure.

**The Golden Rule:** Never move to the next layer until the current one passes its Handoff Criterion. Catch problems early, and you’ll kill the rewrite tax for good.
