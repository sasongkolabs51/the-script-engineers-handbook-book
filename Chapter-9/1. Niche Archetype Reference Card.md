## 1. Niche Archetype Reference Card

**Purpose:** A one‑page cheat sheet that captures the default expectations of the four fundamental creator niches. Use this as a starting point when you need to calibrate for a new audience.

| Niche | Preferred Framework(s) | Primary Emotional Register | Top Hook Archetypes (from Ch. 5) | Pacing Signature | Voice Modifications (vs. your baseline) |
|-------|-------------------------|----------------------------|----------------------------------|------------------|------------------------------------------|
| **Educational / How‑To** | PAS, Sequential Breakdown | Curious, empowering; the viewer should feel progressively more capable. | Curiosity Gap, Specific Promise | Moderate pace; use [Slow emphasis] on key terms, [Text on screen] for steps, minimal hard cuts. | Increase Emotional Temperature warmth slightly; reduce Humor Density by ~30%; maintain Colloquial Fingerprint unchanged. |
| **Reaction / Entertainment** | Hook‑Story‑Offer, 3‑Act Emotional Volatility | High‑variability: swings between shock, critique, amusement, delight. | Contrarian Claim, Relatable Pain | Rapid‑fire; heavy use of [Reaction beat], [Fast energy], [Hard cut]; [Pause 0.5s] for comedic timing. | Increase Humor Density and amplify emotional swings; Sentence Architecture can become even more fragmented; maintain Colloquial Fingerprint. |
| **Story‑Driven / Vlog** | Hero’s Journey, Personal Transformation Arc | Vulnerable, empathetic, reflective; the viewer should feel they’ve shared a genuine human experience. | Open Loop, Relatable Pain | Slow and reflective; frequent [Pause 1.5s], [Slow down], [Whisper]; atmospheric B‑roll, not informational. | Shift Emotional Temperature toward vulnerable end of your range; decrease Humor Density; gentle self‑deprecation only. |
| **Review / Comparison** | Modified PAS, Compare‑Contrast | Authoritative, discerning; the viewer should feel they’re getting an honest, expert assessment. | Contrarian Claim, Curiosity Gap | Crisp and efficient; [Punch] on specs, [Hard cut] between points, [Slow emphasis] on verdict. | Cooler, more analytical register; Humor as sharp observation; Colloquial Fingerprint remains—never become formal. |

**Note:** These are starting calibrations. Your audience may have slightly different expectations. Use these as your first hypothesis and refine with data.
