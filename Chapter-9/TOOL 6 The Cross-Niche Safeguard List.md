## TOOL 6: The Cross-Niche Safeguard List

### *What NEVER changes. Your identity anchor. Paste into every Niche Switch prompt and every Adaptation Blueprint.*

---

**Instructions:** Review your Voice Profile (Chapter 7, Tool 4). Identify the characteristics that are NON-NEGOTIABLE across all niches. These are the elements that make you *you* regardless of content type. If you change these, you're no longer you—you're a different person wearing your name.

---

### My Cross-Niche Safeguards

**ALWAYS (positive constraints — these persist in every niche):**

| # | Safeguard | Why It's Non-Negotiable |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | e.g., "First-person perspective. Always 'I' and 'you.'" | This is how I relate to my audience. Third-person is not me. |
| 2 | e.g., "Contractions always. I'm, don't, can't, won't." | Full forms sound like a different person. |
| 3 | e.g., "Em dashes for asides. Never parentheses." | This is my rhythmic signature. |
| 4 | e.g., "Cultural reference universe: indie tech, 90s films, cooking analogies." | This is my world. It doesn't change per niche. |
| 5 | e.g., "Signature phrases: 'here's the thing,' 'and honestly,' 'the short version.'" | These are my verbal fingerprints. |
| 6 | e.g., "Vulnerability stated plainly. No buildup. No performance." | This is how I handle emotion. In every niche. |
| 7 | e.g., "Audience address: 'you' directly. Never 'folks' or 'guys.'" | This is how I talk to people. |

**NEVER (negative constraints — these are banned in every niche):**

| # | Safeguard | Why It's Non-Negotiable |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | e.g., "Never use 'In today's video' or 'Let's dive in.'" | These are not my phrases. In any context. |
| 2 | e.g., "Never shift to presenter voice or third-person." | I'm always talking TO someone, not AT an audience. |
| 3 | e.g., "Never use motivational poster language." | Not my register. Ever. |
| 4 | e.g., "Never use 'furthermore,' 'moreover,' 'thus,' 'hence.'" | Not my vocabulary. In any niche. |
| 5 | e.g., "Never perform excitement I don't feel." | Authenticity over energy. Always. |
| 6 | e.g., "Never use exclamation points for humor." | My humor is dry. In every niche. |

ALWAYS:
- [Paste your positive safeguards]

- [Paste your negative safeguards]
