## 5. Niche Switch Prompt Template

**Purpose:** A single, integrated prompt that reengineers a script kernel for any target niche. Fill in the bracketed sections using your Niche Adaptation Blueprint, Voice Modification Worksheet, and Pacing Signature Template.

```
You are a Script Engineer adapting content for a specific audience. Below is a universal script kernel—a topic and core points that are not yet tailored to any niche.

UNIVERSAL SCRIPT KERNEL:
Topic: [INSERT TOPIC]
Core Points:
- [Point 1]
- [Point 2]
- [Point 3]
- [Takeaway / Core Insight]

TARGET NICHE: [Niche Name, e.g., Personal Vlog]

ADAPTATION INSTRUCTIONS:

1. HOOK: Use the [Hook Archetype, e.g., Open Loop] hook. [Add any niche‑specific hook instructions from your blueprint, e.g., “Begin with a specific personal story that the viewer can’t walk away from until it resolves.”]

2. STRUCTURE: Shape the script using the [Framework, e.g., Hero’s Journey 7‑beat] structure. Apply these beat priorities: [e.g., “Spend significant time on the Ordeal beat; make the Return with the Elixir feel intimate.”]

3. VOICE: Modify my baseline voice as follows:
[PASTE YOUR VOICE MODIFICATION PARAGRAPH FROM SECTION 3]

4. PACING: Apply this pacing signature throughout:
[PASTE YOUR PACING SIGNATURE PARAGRAPH FROM SECTION 4]

5. GUARDRAILS:
- [Niche‑specific guardrails from your blueprint]
- [Cross‑niche safeguards from your master list]

6. B‑ROLL READY: Insert [B‑roll moment: describe] tokens appropriate for this niche (e.g., atmospheric visuals for vlogs, product shots for reviews). Add [Reaction beat] where the emotional arc demands it.

Output a complete script skeleton (raw prose, no stylistic flourishes yet) that follows all the above. Maintain [HOOK] at the opening and [CTA] at the close. After the skeleton, briefly note which niche‑specific choices you made and why.
```

**Usage:** After the AI generates the skeleton, run your normal Layers 3 (Pacing Injection) and 4 (Voice Overlay) to refine. The heavy lifting of niche adaptation is already done by this prompt.
