### The Modification Prompt Block

Assemble your dial adjustments into a single prompt block to append to your Voice Profile in Layer 4:

```
═══ NICHE VOICE MODIFICATION ═══
Target niche: [niche name]

Apply these adjustments ON TOP of my baseline Voice Profile:
- Sentence Architecture: [your instruction]
- Humor: [your instruction]
- Emotional Temperature: [your instruction]
- Colloquial Fingerprint: [your instruction — usually "UNCHANGED"]

These modifications adjust the ENERGY and DENSITY of my voice,
not its IDENTITY. The core voice remains recognizably mine.
I am the same person speaking in a different register for a
different room.
```
