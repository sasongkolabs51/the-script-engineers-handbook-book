## 7. Niche Blueprint Integration Checklist

After building a blueprint for a new niche, verify it’s fully wired into your system.

- [ ] I have completed the Niche Adaptation Blueprint Builder for this niche.  
- [ ] I have a corresponding Voice Modification paragraph (prompt‑ready).  
- [ ] I have a Pacing Signature paragraph for this niche.  
- [ ] The Niche Switch prompt template is filled in with this niche’s parameters and saved in my Prompt Lab.  
- [ ] My Cross‑Niche Safeguard List is attached to the blueprint.  
- [ ] I have a Layer 1 prompt that asks: “What niche is this video for?” and loads the appropriate blueprint.  
- [ ] I have a sample script kernel to test the Niche Switch prompt.  
- [ ] I know how I’ll gather audience feedback (retention graphs, comments) to validate and refine the blueprint.
