## 10. Niche Adaptation Blueprint Example: Tech Review

*Use this filled‑in example as a model for your own blueprints.*

**Niche Name:** Tech Review  
**Blueprint Version:** 1.0 **Date:** …  

### Primary Framework
Modified PAS or compare‑contrast  

### Default Hook Archetype(s)
☑ Contrarian Claim ☑ Curiosity Gap  

### Voice Modifications
- Sentence Architecture: Keep long‑short oscillation but slightly shorter sentences for clarity.  
- Humor Density & Type: Reduce humor frequency; humor type remains dry, observational.  
- Emotional Temperature: Dial toward authoritative and discerning; warmth appears only in personal verdict moments.  
- Colloquial Fingerprint: Maintain all pet phrases and cultural references; add tech‑specific terms.  

### Pacing Signature
- Default Tempo: Crisp and efficient.  
- Typical Token Density: `[Pause 0.5s]` before key specs; `[B‑roll moment]` every 90–120 seconds.  
- Preferred Tokens: `[Punch]`, `[Hard cut]`, `[Slow emphasis]` on verdict.  
- Visual Conventions: Split‑screen comparisons, screen recordings of interfaces.  

### Niche‑Specific Guardrails
- Never explain a spec without translating it into a user‑experience consequence.  
- Every comparison must end with a clear, concise verdict.  

### Cross‑Niche Safeguards (applied to all niches)
- Always: first‑person, conversational fragments, em dashes for asides.  
- Never: presenter voice, formal transitions, third‑person voice.
