## 5. Daily Identity Anchor Script

**Purpose:** A short spoken read‑aloud that reinforces the layered identity every morning (or before any AI scripting session). Over time, it becomes the default self‑talk.

Read aloud, slowly:

> I am not a keyboard. I am not a word‑generation machine.  
> I am a Visionary who decides what matters.  
> I am a Craftsman who shapes how it’s told.  
> I direct the Engineer—my tools, my AI—to execute the mechanical work.  
> My audience trusts my perspective, my taste, my honesty.  
> No AI can replicate those; no AI can dilute them.  
> The scripts I publish are mine because every creative decision that matters was mine.  
> Today, I will use AI without guilt, because I know exactly where my authorship lives—and it was never in the typing.
