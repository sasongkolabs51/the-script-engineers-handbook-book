## 2. Identity Reframe Canvas

**Purpose:** Create a visual, spatial model of the Three‑Layered Creator Identity. This becomes a one‑glance anchor whenever you need to remember where you stand—and where AI stops.

**Instructions:**  
Draw three concentric circles on a blank page (or use the text template below). Label the innermost circle **Visionary**, the middle circle **Craftsman**, and the outermost circle **Engineer**. Draw a thick, bold line between the Craftsman and Engineer circles and write “AI’s Boundary” across it.

Now, inside each layer, list your specific contributions—the decisions you make that define your authentic creative work. Use the prompts to guide you.
