## 2. Identity Reframe Canvas

*A visual tool you can return to anytime the old guilt surfaces.*

**Instructions:**  
Draw three concentric circles (or use the template below as a mental model). Label and fill them as follows:

- **Innermost circle (Visionary):** List your specific contributions—topics you choose, angles you take, values you uphold, the emotional takeaway you want for your audience, the unique perspective only you have.
- **Middle circle (Craftsman):** List your structural and stylistic decisions—the frameworks you prefer, the pacing style, humor, tone, how you use B‑roll, how you write hooks, how you close videos.
- **Outer circle (Engineer):** List the execution tasks—generating raw drafts, formatting paragraphs, producing multiple variations, filling in connective tissue.
- **Draw a thick line between the Craftsman and Engineer circles and label it “AI’s boundary.”**

**Use this canvas:**  
- Glance at it before you start a scripting session.  
- Revisit it when you feel like the AI “did everything.”  
- Update it every few months as your skills grow.
