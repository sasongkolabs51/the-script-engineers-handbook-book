## 9. Quick Reference: The Three‑Layered Creator Identity

Print this one‑pager and keep it in your workspace.

- **Visionary (Innermost)** – *What to say.*  
  You choose the topic, the angle, the emotional core, the message. This layer is your experience, your values, your unique perspective. AI cannot access it.

- **Craftsman (Middle)** – *How to say it.*  
  You shape the structure, pacing, tone, voice, humor, and audience connection. This layer is your acquired storytelling skill. AI may generate options, but you select, adjust, and approve.

- **Engineer (Outer)** – *Execution.*  
  AI generates raw drafts, variations, and filler. It is a tool for mechanical production, not a source of creative authority. You direct it; you never defer to it.

**The Boundary:** AI operates in the Engineer layer only. Everything your audience loves comes from the inner two layers. That’s why your scripts are authentically yours—not despite the AI, but because you wield it with vision and craft.
