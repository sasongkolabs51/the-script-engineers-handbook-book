## 1. The Zero‑Sum Belief Audit

*Bring hidden thoughts into the light so you can reframe them with your new identity.*

**Instructions:**  
Take a blank page and draw a line down the middle. On the left, write every thought you’ve ever had that frames AI as a threat to your authenticity. Be brutally honest. On the right, rewrite each thought using the Three‑Layered model. A few examples are provided to get you started; complete the rest on your own.

| **Zero‑Sum Thought** | **Reframed Truth (Visionary + Craftsman + Engineer)** |
|----------------------|--------------------------------------------------------|
| “If I use AI, my scripts aren’t really mine.” | “The Visionary chose the topic and the takeaway. The Craftsman shaped the structure and tone. The Engineer handled the raw word generation. All three are me, so the script is mine.” |
| “My audience will feel the difference.” | “My audience responds to vision, craft, and voice. AI only helps with execution speed. None of the things they trust about me were outsourced.” |
| “I am cheating.” | “I am automating execution; the vision and craft decisions are entirely mine. Cheating would mean deceiving my audience about my perspective, and my perspective remains untouched.” |
| “I’m not a real writer if I don’t write every word.” | “A real writer is someone who shapes ideas into impact. I shape the blueprint, the emotional arc, and the final edits. The words are just the raw material I refine.” |
| “Using AI makes me less creative.” | “Creativity lives in the Visionary and Craftsman layers—choosing what to say and how to say it. AI is a faster pencil, not a co‑author.” |
