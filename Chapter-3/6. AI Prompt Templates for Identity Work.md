## 6. AI Prompt Templates for Identity Work

Use these prompts with your AI co‑creator to explore and strengthen your layered identity.

### 6.1 Three‑Layer Audit Assistant

```
I will give you a script that I created with AI assistance. Please analyze it through the lens of the Three‑Layered Creator Identity:

- Visionary: What decisions about topic, angle, emotional core, and values are evident in this script? (These are the “what to say” decisions.)
- Craftsman: What structural, pacing, tone, and stylistic choices can you detect? (These are the “how to say it” decisions.)
- Engineer: What parts of the script could reasonably have been generated as raw material by an AI? (These are the execution‑only elements.)

Do not judge quality. Simply map the script to the three layers and show where creative authority sits. Then, summarize: “The authentic voice in this script comes primarily from the [Visionary/Craftsman] layer because…”

Script:
[PASTE YOUR SCRIPT]
```

### 6.2 Manifesto Refiner

```
I am a creator who uses AI as a tool in my workflow. Below is a draft of my personal Creator Manifesto. Please help me refine it by:

1. Identifying any statements that still carry guilt or self‑doubt and suggesting stronger, more declarative alternatives.
2. Ensuring the manifesto clearly separates my Visionary, Craftsman, and Engineer roles.
3. Suggesting one powerful concluding mantra that captures the essence of guilt‑free AI collaboration.

My draft manifesto:
[PASTE YOUR DRAFT]
```

### 6.3 Guilt Interruption Prompt

Use this in the moment when guilt spikes—just paste your thought and let the AI reframe it.

```
I just had the thought: “[INSERT GUILTY THOUGHT, e.g., I feel like a fraud because the AI wrote that entire transition].” Please reframe this thought using the Three‑Layered Creator Identity (Visionary, Craftsman, Engineer). Remind me where my true authorship lies, and give me a one‑sentence truth I can hold onto right now.
```
