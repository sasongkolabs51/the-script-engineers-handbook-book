## 7. Before/After Identity Card

Keep this quick reference visible. It shows the old zero‑sum identity and the new layered identity side by side.

| Old Identity (Zero‑Sum) | New Identity (Three‑Layered) |
|--------------------------|-------------------------------|
| “I wrote every word or it’s not mine.” | “I made every creative decision that shaped the words. That’s authorship.” |
| “AI is a crutch that makes me less authentic.” | “AI is an Engineer‑layer tool I direct. My authenticity lives in the Visionary and Craftsman layers.” |
| “If the audience knew I used AI, they’d feel cheated.” | “My audience comes for my perspective, not my typing speed. I am honest about my tools; I am honest about my vision.” |
| “Using AI means I’m not a real creator.” | “Real creators orchestrate. The solo‑author myth has always been a myth. I’ve simply made my orchestra explicit.” |
| “I should hide my AI use.” | “I can openly say I use AI for execution, because my value is in direction, not transcription.” |
