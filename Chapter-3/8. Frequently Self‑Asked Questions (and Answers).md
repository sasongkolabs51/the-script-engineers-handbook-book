## 8. Frequently Self‑Asked Questions (and Answers)

*These are the doubts that tend to resurface. Read them as needed.*

**Q: What if the AI writes something better than I could?**  
A: The AI didn’t know it was good. You recognized it. That’s your Visionary and Craftsman layers at work. You selected, refined, and placed that line in the right moment. The taste is yours.

**Q: Am I still a creator if I use AI for 80% of the first draft?**  
A: If you decided what the video is about, how it should feel, which stories to include, and where the emotional peaks go, you’re the creator. The percentage of words typed by you is irrelevant; the percentage of decisions made by you is everything.

**Q: Won’t my audience feel betrayed if they find out?**  
A: Audiences care about value, not production methods. If your videos consistently deliver clear vision, sharp craft, and a consistent voice, they won’t care how you got the words on the page. Many will respect the honesty and the efficiency.

**Q: I still feel a little guilty. Is that normal?**  
A: Yes. Identity shifts take time. The guilt is a habit, not a truth. Every time it surfaces, revisit your manifesto or your Identity Reframe Canvas. Over time, the new neural pathway will strengthen and the old one will fade.
