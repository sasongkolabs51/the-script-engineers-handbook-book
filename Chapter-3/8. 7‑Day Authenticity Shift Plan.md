## 8. 7‑Day Authenticity Shift Plan

A day‑by‑day guide to internalize the Three‑Layered Identity until it feels natural.

**Day 1 – Surface the Guilt**  
- [ ] Complete the Zero‑Sum Belief Audit (Section 1). Fill in at least five old thoughts and reframed truths.  
- [ ] Circle the reframed truth that feels most liberating. Read it aloud three times.

**Day 2 – Visualize the Layers**  
- [ ] Build your Identity Reframe Canvas (Section 2). Draw the concentric circles and fill in your contributions.  
- [ ] Place the canvas where you’ll see it daily. Explain the model to someone—a friend, a partner, or just yourself in the mirror.

**Day 3 – Write Your Manifesto**  
- [ ] Use the Creator Manifesto Builder (Section 3) to draft a complete manifesto.  
- [ ] Read it aloud once in the morning and once before bed. Notice any parts that still feel shaky; refine them.

**Day 4 – Audit Your Best AI Script**  
- [ ] Complete the AI‑Assisted Script Audit (Section 4) on a script you felt conflicted about.  
- [ ] Add your summary paragraph to your manifesto as a permanent case study.

**Day 5 – Anchor the Mantra**  
- [ ] Practice the Daily Identity Anchor Script (Section 5) aloud three times during the day.  
- [ ] Record yourself speaking it and listen back. Hear the conviction in your own voice.

**Day 6 – Use the AI Prompts**  
- [ ] Run the “Three‑Layer Audit Assistant” prompt on a recent script.  
- [ ] Run the “Guilt Interruption” prompt on any lingering doubtful thought.  
- [ ] Notice how the AI itself can reinforce your authorship when prompted correctly.

**Day 7 – Live the Identity**  
- [ ] Create a new script using AI, start to finish, with the layered identity fully activated.  
- [ ] After you finish, reflect: “Did guilt arise? If so, I returned to my manifesto and canvas. The script is mine.”  
- [ ] Write one sentence to close the week: “I now know my authenticity lives in my ________, not in my ________.”
