## 6. Pacing Mastery Lab Checklist

*Follow these four exercises in order to go from token awareness to full‑script rhythmic scoring.*

☐ **1. Pacing Injection Wizard Retrofit**  
☐ Selected a previously recorded script that felt flat despite good content.  
☐ Ran it through the standalone pacing injection prompt (use the fifteen tokens; add B‑roll every 90–120 seconds).  
☐ Read the retrofitted version aloud alongside the original. Noted the difference.  

☐ **2. Build Your First Rhythm Grid**  
☐ Chose a frequently‑used framework (PAS, Hero’s Journey, Hook‑Story‑Offer).  
☐ Drew the beat sequence on a horizontal axis, emotional intensity on the vertical.  
☐ Plotted the emotional arc as you intuitively feel it.  
☐ Translated each inflection point into a tempo instruction (`[Fast energy]` at rises, `[Slow down]` at falls, `[Pause]` at peaks and valleys).  
☐ Saved as a reusable template (e.g., “PAS Rhythm Grid v1.0”).  

☐ **3. Run the Full Pacing‑Integrated Chain**  
☐ Layer 1: selected framework and hook archetype; included hook‑tempo instruction.  
☐ Layer 2: pasted structural skeleton prompt with beat‑specific tempo map from your rhythm grid.  
☐ Layer 3: ran pacing injection with token vocabulary, tempo map, and B‑Roll Ready instructions.  
☐ Layer 4: applied voice overlay, preserving all pacing tokens.  
☐ Read the final script aloud and recorded yourself.  
☐ Listened back, marked moments where rhythm felt unnatural.  
☐ Adjusted the tempo map and reran Layers 3–4 until the recording felt like a natural performance.  

☐ **4. Advanced Adaptation: Niche‑Specific Pacing Signatures**  
☐ Defined your niche’s default tempo, typical token density, and visual conventions.  
☐ Created a “Pacing Signature” addendum and placed it in your Voice Profile and Layer 3 template.
