## 8. Troubleshooting Quick Reference

| **Symptom** | **Probable Cause** | **Fix** |
|-------------|-------------------|---------|
| AI ignores pacing tokens | Tokens blending into the text | Ensure tokens are all caps, square brackets; prompt explicitly says “Preserve all tokens exactly as written.” |
| Rhythm feels rushed overall | Not enough pauses | Increase frequency of pause tokens, especially after key statements. |
| Rhythm feels choppy | Too many hard pauses | Replace some `[Pause]` tokens with `[Slow down]` or softer transitions. |
| Voice overlay removes tokens | Layer 4 prompt doesn’t protect tokens | Add to Layer 4: “Maintain all pacing tokens and their placement exactly as they appear. Do not alter, remove, or rephrase them.” |
| Visual cues are generic | B‑Roll prompt too vague | Add specificity directives: “include brand names, interface elements, specific objects.” |
