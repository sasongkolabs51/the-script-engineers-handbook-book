## 2. Rhythm Grid Builder Worksheet

**Purpose:** Turn your chosen narrative framework into an emotional tempo map. This map guides exactly where tokens belong.

### Step 1 – Draw Your Grid

Take a blank page (or the space below). Draw a horizontal axis with your beat numbers from your framework (e.g., Beat 1–6 for PAS). Draw a vertical axis labeled “Emotional Intensity” (low to high).

**Example for PAS (6 beats):**
- Beat 1 (Problem Identification) → Medium‑High (relatable but charged)
- Beat 2 (Agitation Layer 1) → Medium‑High, slightly rising
- Beat 3 (Agitation Layer 2) → High
- Beat 4 (Solution Introduction) → Low‑Medium (emotional exhale)
- Beat 5 (Solution Expansion) → Rising again, empowered
- Beat 6 (Empowered Future) → Warm, confident Medium

**Example for PAS:**

| Beat | Emotional Intensity | Tempo / Pacing Instruction |
|------|---------------------|----------------------------|
| 1 (Problem ID) | Medium‑High | `[Fast energy]` to hook, then natural pace; end with `[Pause 1.5s]` |
| 2 (Agitation 1) | Rising | Begin `[Build speed]` across the beat; end with `[Pause 1s]` |
| 3 (Agitation 2) | High | `[Fast energy]` throughout; insert `[Punch]` on a startling stat; end with `[Pause 3s]` |
| 4 (Solution Intro) | Low‑Medium | `[Slow down]` for the insight; `[Whisper]` if personal; `[Pause 1.5s]` after |
| 5 (Solution Expansion) | Rising, Empowered | `[Build speed]` as steps unfold; `[Text on screen]` for key steps |
| 6 (Empowered Future) | Warm Medium | `[Fade out energy]`; final `[Pause 2s]` before CTA |

### Step 4 – Compile Your Tempo Map

Write a single paragraph that describes the full rhythmic arc, incorporating the above instructions. This will go directly into your Layer 3 pacing prompt.

*Example tempo map paragraph:*  
“The script follows the PAS framework. Beat 1: open with `[Fast energy]` hook, natural pace, end with `[Pause 1.5s]`. Beat 2: `[Build speed]` through daily frustrations, end `[Pause 1s]`. Beat 3: `[Fast energy]` throughout, `[Punch]` on a devastating stat, end with `[Pause 3s]` and `[Reaction beat]`. Beat 4: `[Slow down]`, `[Whisper]` on the personal insight, `[Pause 2s]`. Beat 5: `[Build speed]` through steps, `[Text on screen]` for each step. Beat 6: `[Fade out energy]`, final `[Pause 2s]` before CTA. Insert `[B‑roll moment]` every 90‑120 seconds.”
