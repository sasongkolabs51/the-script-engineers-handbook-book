## 1. Pacing Token Reference Card

*Keep this list of fifteen tokens nearby when you build your Layer 3 pacing injection prompts. They are your rhythmic vocabulary.*

| **Category** | **Token** | **What It Does** |
|--------------|-----------|------------------|
| **Pause** | `[Pause 0.5s]` | Micro‑pause; separates a setup from a punchline. |
| **Pause** | `[Pause 1.5s]` | Standard beat; lets a significant statement land. |
| **Pause** | `[Pause 3s]` | Dramatic silence; after a revelation or before a tonal shift. |
| **Speed** | `[Fast energy]` | Rapid, excited section; used in hooks or when building momentum. |
| **Speed** | `[Slow down]` | Deceleration for emphasis; often in confessions or solution intros. |
| **Speed** | `[Build speed]` | Crescendo effect over a sentence or two. |
| **Emphasis** | `[Whisper]` | Secret, vulnerable admission, or conspiratorial aside. |
| **Emphasis** | `[Punch]` | Key word in a punchline or core number in a statistic. |
| **Emphasis** | `[Slow emphasis]` | Crucial term to savor; product name, central concept. |
| **Visual** | `[B‑roll moment: describe X]` | Where a visual cutaway should happen and what it should show. |
| **Visual** | `[Reaction beat]` | Presenter pauses to react facially; used after vulnerable lines. |
| **Visual** | `[Text on screen: “quote”]` | A key line reinforced visually. |
| **Transition** | `[Hard cut]` | Abrupt tonal or topical shift; often comedic. |
| **Transition** | `[Fade out energy]` | Final moments leading into the CTA. |
| **Transition** | `[Beat drop]` | Moment after a long pause when energy returns. |

**Usage rule:** Tokens are always inline, inside square brackets, exactly as written.
