## 9. Daily Pacing Identity Anchor

Read aloud before your pacing injection or recording session.

> Words are only half the performance. The other half is rhythm.  
> I don’t just write scripts—I score them for the human voice.  
> Pauses, speed shifts, whispers, punchlines—these are my instruments.  
> My rhythm grid maps the emotional arc, and my tokens bring it to life.  
> When I read my script aloud, it breathes. It swells and falls. It lands.  
> The viewer doesn’t notice the pacing; they just feel the ride.  
> Today, my scripts will not be flat. They will have a pulse.
