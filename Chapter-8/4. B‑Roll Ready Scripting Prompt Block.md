## 4. B‑Roll Ready Scripting Prompt Block

*Copy this block directly into your Layer 3 pacing injection prompt to add visual cadence instructions.*

“Insert a `[B‑roll moment: describe]` every 90 to 120 seconds of spoken content. Each B‑roll moment must describe a specific visual that reinforces or adds context to the immediately preceding sentence. Do not repeat visuals. The description must be concrete enough for an editor or stock footage search (include brand names, interface elements, specific objects).

Insert a `[Reaction beat]` after every personal confession or vulnerable statement. This is a moment where the presenter looks at the camera silently for one to two seconds, letting the emotion register.”
