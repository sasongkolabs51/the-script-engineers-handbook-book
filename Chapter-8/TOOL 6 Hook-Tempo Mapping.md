## TOOL 6: Hook-Tempo Mapping

### *Which pacing tokens fit which hook archetype. Plug into your Hook Generation Machine (Chapter 5, Tool 6).*

---

| Hook Archetype | Natural Tempo | Recommended Tokens | Rhythm Pattern |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Curiosity Gap** | Sharp, slightly fast. Drop the fact, then pause. | [Fast energy] on the fact → [Pause 1.5s] → [Slow emphasis] on the key word | "The number was [Punch] zero. [Pause 1.5s] And the reason..." |
| **Contrarian Claim** | Blunt, confident. State it. Let it land. | Statement at medium speed → [Pause 1.5s] → [Slow down] for the grounding sentence | "[Contrarian claim stated plainly]. [Pause 1.5s] [Slow down] And I don't say that lightly..." |
| **Relatable Pain** | Slow, warm, immersive. Drop into the scene. | [Slow down] from the first sentence. [Pause 0.5s] after the sensory detail. No [Fast energy]. | "[Slow down] It's 1:47 a.m. [Pause 0.5s] The coffee's cold. The cursor is blinking..." |
| **Specific Promise** | Confident, medium-fast. State the outcome clearly. | Medium tempo. [Punch] on the key number. [Pause 0.5s] before the proof anchor. | "You'll cut your planning time from [Punch] three hours to [Punch] forty-five minutes. [Pause 0.5s] I've done it every Sunday for 14 weeks." |
| **Identity Challenge** | Warm → slightly uncomfortable. Name the identity, then the gap. | [Slow down] for the identity naming → [Pause 1s] → medium speed for the undermining behavior | "[Slow down] You're the kind of person who values deep focus. [Pause 1s] And you also check Slack every seven minutes." |
| **Open Loop** | Fast, breathless. Drop into the story mid-action. | [Fast energy] or [Build speed] for the scene → [Pause 1.5s] at the cliffhanger → stop. | "[Build speed] I was 40 minutes into the call when I realized— [Pause 1.5s] —the file I'd sent was the wrong version." |

---

### How to Use This

Add a tempo instruction to your Hook Generation Machine prompt (Chapter 5, Tool 6):

```
HOOK TEMPO INSTRUCTION:
The hook should be delivered at [tempo from table above].
Use the following tokens: [tokens from table above].
The rhythm pattern should follow: [pattern from table above].
```

This ensures your hook is rhythmically scored from the moment it's generated—not retrofitted later.
