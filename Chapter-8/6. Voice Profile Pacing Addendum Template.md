## 6. Voice Profile Pacing Addendum Template

Add this section to your existing Voice Profile document (from Chapter 7). It connects your natural speaking rhythm to the AI’s token placement.

```
### Pacing Signature (add to Voice Profile v1.0)

- Natural speech tempo: [e.g., moderately fast, with frequent short pauses for emphasis]
- I tend to accelerate when [e.g., excited about data or making a point]
- I decelerate and drop volume when [e.g., sharing something vulnerable or personal]
- My typical pause length after a punchline: [e.g., about 1 second, never longer]
- I use visual reinforcements heavily / sparingly (circle one)
- I almost never use: [e.g., dramatic slow‑motion speech, shouting, hard cuts in serious content]
```

When you generate pacing, the token placement should feel like an extension of your natural rhythm.
