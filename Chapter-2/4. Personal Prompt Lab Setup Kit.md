## 4. Personal Prompt Lab Setup Kit

*This is the blueprint for your deliberate practice space. Set it up now, not later.*

**Step 1: Create the physical/digital space.**  
Open a blank document, note, or a new ChatGPT conversation thread. Title it: **My Prompt Lab**.

**Step 2: Create the three permanent sections.**  
Copy these headings into your lab:

### SECTION 1: Structural Experiments
*Test hooks, stakes, tension loops, payoff structures.*

### SECTION 2: Voice Experiments
*Test voice profiles, tone modifications, first-person vs. third-person, etc.*

### SECTION 3: Pacing Experiments
*Test pacing tokens, rhythm variations, sentence lengths, pauses.*

**Step 3: Plan your first experiment right now.**  
Fill out this mini-brief:

- **Experiment Name:** ________________________________________  
- **Which Section?** (Structural / Voice / Pacing): ________________________________  
- **Topic:** ________________________________________  
- **Variable I’m Testing:** ________________________________________  
- **Prompt I’ll Use (draft):**  
________________________________________________________________________________________  
________________________________________________________________________________________  
- **What I’ll Observe:** (How does the output change? What’s the emotional or retention difference?)  
________________________________________________________________________________________  
- **I’ll run this experiment at:** (specific time today/tomorrow) ________
