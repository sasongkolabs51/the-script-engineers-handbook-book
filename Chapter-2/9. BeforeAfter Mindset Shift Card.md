## 9. Before/After Mindset Shift Card

**Purpose:** A quick reference to keep the new paradigm front and center during your work.

| Old Secret-Seeker Mindset | New Craftsman Mindset |
|---------------------------|------------------------|
| “I need to find the perfect template for this video.” | “I know the narrative functions my script needs. Let me engineer a prompt that builds them.” |
| “I’ll save this and maybe use it later.” | “I’ll extract the principle and test it in my lab right now.” |
| “There must be a secret prompt everyone else knows.” | “Mastery is not a secret; it’s a practice. My lab is where I build it.” |
| “My swipe file is my greatest asset.” | “My understanding of structure is my greatest asset. The lab log is its proof.” |
| “I’m falling behind because I don’t have the latest templates.” | “I’m moving ahead because I’m deepening my craft every week.” |
