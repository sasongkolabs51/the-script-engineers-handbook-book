## 10. 7-Day Secret-Seeker Detox Plan

A day-by-day guide to flush out the old habit and install the new one.

**Day 1 – Face the Hoard**  
- [ ] Complete the Vault Inventory Audit (Section 1).  
- [ ] Write down the number of unused items. Let yourself feel the discomfort—it’s the fuel for change.

**Day 2 – Calculate the Cost**  
- [ ] Fill out the Output Gap Calculator (Section 2).  
- [ ] Write the “ROI Gap sentence” and tape it to your wallet or credit card.

**Day 3 – Extract Five Principles**  
- [ ] Use the Principle Extraction Worksheet (Section 3) on five templates from your audit.  
- [ ] For each, create a structural prompt for your niche and test it briefly.  
- [ ] Note which principle feels most immediately useful.

**Day 4 – Build Your Lab**  
- [ ] Set up your Personal Prompt Lab (Section 4).  
- [ ] Run one full experiment in the “Structural” section using the most useful principle from Day 3.  
- [ ] Log the result in your Experiment Log (Section 5).

**Day 5 – Voice Experiment**  
- [ ] Run an experiment in the “Voice” section: take the same hook and generate it with three different voice profiles.  
- [ ] Observe which feels most authentic. Add a note to your Voice-DNA toolkit (later chapter preview).  
- [ ] Log it.

**Day 6 – Write Your Pledge**  
- [ ] Complete the Secret-Seeker Pledge Builder (Section 6) in your own words.  
- [ ] Place it physically near your workspace and digitally in your Prompt Lab.  
- [ ] Read the Daily Craft Identity Anchor (Section 7) aloud before your next creative session.

**Day 7 – Teach the Shift**  
- [ ] Explain the Secret-Seeker’s Plateau and the Principle Extraction habit to a creator friend (or record a voice memo for yourself).  
- [ ] Teaching solidifies your own shift. Notice how naturally the concepts now come to you.  
- [ ] Commit: “Next time I see a ‘viral hook formula’ online, I’ll extract the principle and test it within ten minutes—or not engage at all.”

You’ve now dismantled the template‑hoarding identity and replaced it with a workshop mindset that will make every technique in the rest of this book stick. Keep the Before/After Mindset Card visible, and let your Prompt Lab become the most visited space in your creative workflow.
