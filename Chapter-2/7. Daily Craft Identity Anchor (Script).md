## 7. Daily Craft Identity Anchor (Script)

**Purpose:** A short spoken anchor to read before you open any AI tool or browse any social platform. It locks in the craftsman identity and weakens the collector’s reflex.

Read aloud, ideally in the morning:

> I am not a treasure hunter. I am a workshop builder.  
> My skill isn’t measured by the size of my vault—it’s measured by how deeply I understand the principles behind a great script.  
> Every template I encounter is a chance to extract a principle and test it in my lab.  
> I don’t “try out” other people’s prompts; I reverse‑engineer them and then build my own.  
> My Prompt Lab is my gym. My log is my proof. My scripts are my craft.  
> Today, I’ll do one deliberate practice rep—not because it’s quick, but because it’s permanent.
