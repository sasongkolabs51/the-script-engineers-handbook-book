## 8. Principle Extraction Protocol (Cut‑Out Card)

*Print this or keep it in your digital notes. Use it every single time you encounter a “magic prompt” or “viral hook template.”*

**Step 1: Identify the structural job.**  
What narrative function does this template serve? (Hook? Stakes? Tension loop? Payoff? CTA?)

**Step 2: Name the psychological principle.**  
What human trigger is being pulled? (Curiosity gap? Social proof? Vicarious benefit? Pattern break? Personal identification?)

**Step 3: Rewrite as a structural command for your niche.**  
“Instruct the AI to [mechanism] by [specific structural element], not [original words].”

Example:  
Original: “I tried X so you don’t have to”  
Principle: Vicarious benefit + implied personal cost = instant trust  
Structural prompt: “Open with a sentence that signals I personally tested a risky or expensive tool so the viewer doesn’t have to, using a specific detail that implies time or money spent.”

**Step 4: Test in your Prompt Lab within ten minutes.**  
Run one mini‑experiment. Observe. Learn. Own it.

**These templates are designed to dismantle the Secret-Seeker’s Plateau and replace it with a workshop mindset. Use them until the idea of passively collecting a template feels like a waste of your most valuable asset: your own growing craft.**
