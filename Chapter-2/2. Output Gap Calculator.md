## 2. Output Gap Calculator

**Purpose:** Attach a financial and temporal number to the Secret-Seeker’s Plateau. This converts “vague stagnation” into a motivator you can feel.

**Part A – Investment**
- Money spent on template bundles, prompt courses, “secret” cheat sheets, and swipe‑file subscriptions (last 2 years): $________  
- Estimated total time spent searching, saving, and organizing these assets (hours): ________  
- Mental energy drained (subjective, but note: constant context‑switching and “hope disappointment”): ________

**Part B – Outcome**
- Approximate improvement in your key metric over the same 2 years:  
  Average view duration increase: ________%  
  Subscriber conversion rate improvement: ________%  
  Creative satisfaction (1–10 now vs. then): ________ → ________  
- Number of scripts you’re genuinely proud of that came from a collected template: ________

**Part C – The ROI Gap**
On a scale of 1–10, how would you rate the return on your “secret-seeking” investment? ________

**One sentence that captures the gap:**  
“I spent $________ and ________ hours collecting ________ items, and my actual script quality moved from ____ to ____.”

**Commitment:** The next time I feel the urge to buy a new “magic prompt,” I will re‑read that sentence before opening my wallet.
