## 8. AI Prompt Template: Principle Extraction Assistant

**Purpose:** Use your AI co‑creator to help you extract the structural principle from any template or hook you encounter. This turns the machine into your analytical partner, not just a script generator.

```
I will give you a template, hook formula, or prompt that I’ve found. Please analyze it and answer three questions:

1. What structural job is this template doing? (e.g., opening a curiosity gap, escalating stakes, delivering social proof, creating a tension loop)
2. What is the underlying psychological or narrative principle that makes it work? (explain the mechanism, not just the surface words)
3. Rewrite this template as a STRUCTURAL PROMPT for [MY NICHE, e.g., tech reviews, productivity, personal finance]. The new prompt must capture the same mechanism without copying the original text. It should instruct the AI to generate the principle, not the exact wording.

Template/hook:
[PASTE THE TEMPLATE OR HOOK HERE]
```

After the AI responds, critically review its answers. Adjust the structural prompt to fit your voice. Then immediately test it in your Prompt Lab.
