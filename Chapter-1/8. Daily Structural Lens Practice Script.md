## 8. Daily Structural Lens Practice Script

**Purpose:** A short, spoken mantra to read before you open your AI tool. It anchors the new identity until it becomes automatic.

> I’m not looking for “vibes.” I’m looking for load‑bearing narrative beams.  
> If the hook doesn’t surprise me, it’s not a hook—it’s a topic sentence.  
> If I can’t feel the stakes in my gut, the viewer won’t either.  
> If there’s no unanswered question, there’s no reason to watch past the intro.  
> Every vague adjective I add to a prompt is a tiny act of sabotage.  
> Today, I’ll prompt like an engineer: Hook. Stakes. Tension Loop. The rest follows.
