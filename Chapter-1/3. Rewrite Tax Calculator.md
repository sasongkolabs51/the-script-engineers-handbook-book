## 3. Rewrite Tax Calculator

*Reveal how much of your editing grind is really structural surgery—work that could be eliminated by engineering the architecture in the prompt itself.*

**Step 1: Log your editing session** for one script. Use a timer or your best honest estimate.

| **Editing Activity** | **Time Spent** | **Type** (Structural / Cosmetic) |
|----------------------|----------------|----------------------------------|
| Rewriting the hook because it was a topic sentence, not a magnet | | Structural |
| Punching up flat stakes or inserting urgency | | Structural |
| Restructuring the payoff to actually satisfy the tension | | Structural |
| Fixing word choice, tone, or phrasing to sound more like you | | Cosmetic |
| Adjusting transitions or flow | | Cosmetic (unless rebuilding missing transitions) |
| *(Add your own rows as needed)* | | |

**Step 2: Calculate your tax rate.**

Total editing time: ________ minutes  
Total structural repair time: ________ minutes  

**Structural Tax Rate = (Structural Time ÷ Total Time) × 100 = ________%**

If your rate is above 50%, you’re not editing—you’re re‑engineering on the back end. Every percentage point above 50 is a direct invitation to move that structural command into your prompt.
