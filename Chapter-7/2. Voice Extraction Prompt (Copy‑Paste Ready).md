## 2. Voice Extraction Prompt (Copy‑Paste Ready)

*Send this prompt to the AI for each sample. The AI will return a structured voice specification.*

**Prompt:**  
“Analyze the following text for voice patterns. Identify:  
(1) Sentence architecture — average sentence length, variation pattern, use of fragments, and specific punctuation habits (em dashes, parentheticals, starting sentences with ‘And’ or ‘But’).  
(2) Humor frequency and type — note every joke or playful aside, its mechanism (self‑deprecation, exaggeration, understatement, cultural reference, wordplay), and where it appears.  
(3) Emotional temperature — baseline emotional register and how it shifts across the text (e.g., warm, skeptical, vulnerable, analytical).  
(4) Colloquial fingerprint — repeated words, phrases, idioms, words you never use, grammatical quirks (contractions, passive voice), and the cultural universe referenced.  
Output a structured voice specification.”
