# Chapter 7 Companion Toolkit  
*Voice-DNA Lockdown — Training AI to Sound Like You, Every Time*

This toolkit replaces the vague hope of “write in my voice” with a precise, calibrated, and permanently reusable Voice Profile. You’ll deconstruct your unique sound into four measurable dimensions, run a calibration loop that turns your best writing into an AI‑ready specification, and integrate it as a non‑negotiable layer of your workflow. By the end, tone‑drift will be a ghost you no longer need to fear.
