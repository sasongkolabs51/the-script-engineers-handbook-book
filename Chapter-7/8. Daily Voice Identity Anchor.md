## 8. Daily Voice Identity Anchor

Read aloud before you apply the Voice Overlay layer, or when you’re feeling the old doubt creep in.

> My voice is not a vague vibe I hope the AI guesses. It is a specification I engineered.  
> Sentence architecture, humor type, emotional temperature, colloquial fingerprint—these are my levers.  
> I calibrated them from my own best writing, and I can recalibrate them any time I grow.  
> The AI doesn’t “sound like me” by accident. It sounds like me because I gave it a blueprint, not a wish.  
> When I read the output and it feels like something I’d say, that’s not luck—that’s the Voice Profile working.  
> Today, my scripts will sound like me from the first word to the last. That’s not a hope. That’s a spec.
