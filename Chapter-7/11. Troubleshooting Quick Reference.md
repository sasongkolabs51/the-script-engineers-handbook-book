## 11. Troubleshooting Quick Reference

| **Problem** | **Likely Cause** | **Fix** |
|-------------|------------------|---------|
| Voice drifts after a few paragraphs | Profile lacks constraint density | Add more specific “never use” words, tighten fragment frequency, include a concrete example sentence |
| Jokes fall flat or feel forced | Humor type too abstract | Add an actual joke you’ve used in a video, with a note explaining why it works, directly into the profile |
| Emotional tone feels uniform | Emotional Temperature range too narrow or baseline too neutral | Specify at least two distinct shifts (e.g., “from skeptical to warm,” “from analytical to vulnerable”) |
| Voice sounds formal despite profile | Interference from earlier layers | Check Layer 2 beat scripts for formal language; rewrite any “thus,” “therefore,” “moreover” |
| AI ignores a specific constraint | Constraint worded too broadly | Replace “Don’t be formal” with “Never use ‘therefore,’ ‘thus,’ ‘moreover,’ or ‘in conclusion’” |
| Voice feels “off” on certain topics | Colloquial Fingerprint missing topic‑specific vocabulary | Add niche‑specific terms you naturally use and those you’d never say |
