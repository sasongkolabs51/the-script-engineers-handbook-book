### Step 3: The Diagnosis

For each "off" moment, identify which dimension failed:

| Line / Section That Sounds Wrong | What's Wrong (describe the feeling) | Failing Dimension | What to Adjust in Profile |
|---|---|---|---|
| e.g., "It is therefore essential to consider..." | Too formal. I'd never say "therefore." | Colloquial Fingerprint (banned word) | Add "therefore" to banned list. Add "so" as the replacement. |
| e.g., Three 20-word sentences in a row | Too dense. I'd break this up with a fragment. | Sentence Architecture | Tighten the rhythm instruction: "Never three sentences over 15 words in a row." |
| e.g., A joke with an exclamation point | I don't signal jokes. They land dry. | Humor (delivery) | Add: "Never use exclamation points for humor. Jokes are stated flatly." |
| e.g., "I'm SO excited to share this!" | I never gush. This is motivational speaker energy. | Emotional Temperature (never) | Add to "Never" list: "Never use 'SO' as an intensifier. Never perform excitement." |
