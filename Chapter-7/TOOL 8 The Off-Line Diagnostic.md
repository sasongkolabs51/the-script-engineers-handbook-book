## TOOL 8: The "Off-Line" Diagnostic

### *A specific line sounds wrong. Trace it to the failing dimension. Fix the profile, not just the line.*

---

### The Diagnostic Protocol

**The line that sounds wrong (paste it):**

> "_______________________________________________"

**What's wrong? (Describe the feeling, not the fix):**

> _______________________________________________
> (e.g., "Too formal," "Too energetic," "Not funny in the right way," "Sounds like a textbook," "Too generic — anyone could have written this")

---

### Trace to the Dimension

| Dimension | Diagnostic Question | Is This the Culprit? |
|---|---|---|
| **Sentence Architecture** | Is the sentence too long? Too short? Missing a fragment where I'd naturally pause? Wrong punctuation? Starting with a word I'd never start with? | ☐ Yes ☐ No |
| **Humor** | Is there a joke where I wouldn't put one? Is the joke the wrong TYPE? Is it signaled (exclamation point, "haha") when I deliver dry? Is it missing where I'd naturally add one? | ☐ Yes ☐ No |
| **Emotional Temperature** | Is the tone too warm? Too cold? Too excited? Too flat? Is it gushing where I'd be understated? Is it clinical where I'd be warm? | ☐ Yes ☐ No |
| **Colloquial Fingerprint** | Is there a banned word? A missing contraction? A formal word where I'd use a simple one? A cultural reference outside my universe? A phrase I'd never say? | ☐ Yes ☐ No |

### The Principle

> Don't just fix the line. Fix the PROFILE. The line is a symptom. The profile is the system. If one line drifted, others will too—on this script and every future one. Update the constraint. Re-run Layer 4. The fix should be permanent.

---

### Common "Off" Patterns and Their Dimension

| What You See | What It Means | Dimension | Profile Fix |
|---|---|---|---|
| "It is therefore essential..." | Formal connector slipped in | Colloquial Fingerprint | Add "therefore," "thus," "hence" to banned list. Add "so" as replacement. |
| Three 20+ word sentences in a row | Rhythm constraint too loose | Sentence Architecture | Add: "Never three consecutive sentences over 15 words." |
| "I'm SO excited to share this!" | Emotional temperature too high | Emotional Temperature | Add to "Never": "Never use 'SO' as intensifier. Never perform excitement." |
| A pun with an exclamation point | Humor delivery wrong | Humor | Add: "Jokes delivered flat. No exclamation points. No 'haha.'" |
| "Folks, let me tell you..." | Wrong audience address | Colloquial Fingerprint | Add "folks" to banned. Specify: "Address as 'you.' Never 'folks/guys/everyone.'" |
| A paragraph with zero personality | Voice not applied to this section | All / prompt constraint | Add: "Every paragraph must contain at least one element from the Colloquial Fingerprint (a signature word, a fragment, a cultural reference)." |
| Sudden shift to third person | Perspective constraint missing | Sentence Architecture | Add: "First person always. 'I' and 'you.' Never shift to 'one' or 'the viewer' or 'the audience' as subject." |
| Motivational language in the CTA | Emotional temperature wrong for CTA | Emotional Temperature | Add CTA-specific instruction: "CTA tone: low-pressure, slightly sheepish. An invitation. Never 'SMASH that button.' Never 'You deserve this!'" |
