## 1. Voice Dimension Extraction Worksheet

**Purpose:** Mine your own writing for the concrete patterns that make your voice distinctive. Do this with three separate samples—ideally from scripts you’ve recorded and love.

**Instructions:** For each sample, read it aloud first. Then fill out the prompts below as if you were a forensic linguist. Be brutally specific. Broad adjectives (“conversational”) are useless; concrete observations (“average sentence length is 11 words, with spikes of 4‑word fragments after longer explanations”) are gold.
