## 12. Voice‑DNA Lockdown Summary Card

*The complete process in four lines.*

1. **Extract** — Feed three authentic writing samples to the AI using the extraction prompt.  
2. **Profile** — Synthesize the stable patterns into a one‑page Voice Profile organized by the four dimensions.  
3. **Test & Refine** — Apply the profile to unvoiced scripts, read aloud, tighten the dimensions that feel off.  
4. **Deploy** — Paste the profile into your Layer 4 prompt. Use it unchanged. Re‑calibrate only when your voice evolves.
