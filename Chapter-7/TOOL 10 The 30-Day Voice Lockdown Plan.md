## TOOL 10: The 30-Day Voice Lockdown Plan

### *Build the profile. Test it. Refine it. Make it permanent. Thirty days to a voice that never drifts.*

---

### Week 1: Extraction + Build (Days 1–7)

| Day | Action | Time | Done? |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Collect three writing samples (Tool 1). Read them aloud. Note your gut reactions. | 20 min | ☐ |
| 2 | Run the Voice Extraction Prompt (Tool 2) on Sample 1. Read the output. Note what's accurate and what's wrong. | 15 min | ☐ |
| 3 | Run extraction on Samples 2 and 3. Compare all three outputs. Identify stable patterns. | 25 min | ☐ |
| 4 | Complete the Four-Dimension Deep-Dive Worksheets (Tool 3). Interpret the extraction data. Make deliberate choices. | 40 min | ☐ |
| 5 | Assemble your Voice Profile v1.0 (Tool 4). Write it carefully. This is your creative identity in specification form. | 30 min | ☐ |
| 6 | Run the first calibration test (Tool 5, Steps 1–2). Use a real Layer 3 output. Read aloud. Note "off" moments. | 25 min | ☐ |
| 7 | Diagnose and refine (Tool 5, Steps 3–5). Update the profile. Re-test. | 30 min | ☐ |

---

### Week 2: Calibration + Integration (Days 8–14)

| Day | Action | Time | Done? |
|---|---|---|---|
| 8 | Run a second calibration test on a DIFFERENT script (different topic, different framework). | 25 min | ☐ |
| 9 | Diagnose and refine. Update profile to v1.1 if needed. | 20 min | ☐ |
| 10 | Run a third calibration test on a THIRD script type (e.g., if first two were educational, test a personal story). | 25 min | ☐ |
| 11 | Final refinement. Lock Voice Profile v1.0 (or v1.1). Save as permanent template. | 15 min | ☐ |
| 12 | Integrate as permanent Layer 4 (Tool 6). Replace all ad hoc voice instructions in your Layered Prompting Playbook. | 10 min | ☐ |
| 13 | Produce a full script using the complete chain with your permanent Layer 4. Time yourself. | 50 min | ☐ |
| 14 | **Reflection:** How much manual voice editing did I need? (Target: under 2 minutes.) What still feels slightly off? | 10 min | ☐ |

---

### Week 3: Stress Testing (Days 15–21)

| Day | Action | Time | Done? |
|---|---|---|---|
| 15 | Produce a script on a topic you've NEVER covered before. Does the voice hold on unfamiliar territory? | 50 min | ☐ |
| 16 | Produce a script in a different FORMAT (e.g., if you usually do 10-min essays, try a 3-min quick take). Does the voice adapt without breaking? | 40 min | ☐ |
| 17 | Produce a script with a different FRAMEWORK (if you've been using PAS, try Hero's Journey). Does the voice survive the structural shift? | 50 min | ☐ |
| 18 | Run the Voice Fidelity Scorecard (Tool 7) on all three scripts from Days 15–17. Note patterns. | 20 min | ☐ |
| 19 | If any script scored below 4/5, diagnose with Tool 8. Update the profile. | 20 min | ☐ |
| 20 | Test the profile on your CTA specifically. Write 3 different CTAs using the profile. Do they all sound like you? Or does the AI shift into "YouTuber voice"? | 20 min | ☐ |
| 21 | **Reflection:** Is the voice consistent across topics, formats, and frameworks? Where does it still wobble? | 10 min | ☐ |

---

### Week 4: Permanence + Evolution (Days 22–30)

| Day | Action | Time | Done? |
|---|---|---|---|
| 22–26 | Normal production. Every script uses the permanent Layer 4 prompt. No exceptions. Track your Fidelity Score for each. | 5 min per script | ☐ |
| 27 | Review your Fidelity Log (Tool 7). Are scores consistently 4–5? Is manual editing under 2 minutes? | 15 min | ☐ |
| 28 | Read your Voice Profile aloud. Does it still feel accurate? Has your voice shifted in the last month? Note any updates for v2.0 (future). | 15 min | ☐ |
| 29 | Teach the four-dimension model to someone. Explain Sentence Architecture, Humor, Emotional Temperature, and Colloquial Fingerprint in your own words. | 20 min | ☐ |
| 30 | **Final evaluation:** Compare a script from before this chapter (with "write in my voice" prompting) to your most recent script (with the Voice Profile). Read both aloud. Feel the difference. Write one sentence about what changed. | 15 min | ☐ |

---

### The Ongoing Practice (After Day 30)

> **Every script:** Paste the permanent Layer 4 prompt. No ad hoc voice instructions. Ever.
>
> **Every 10 scripts:** Run the Fidelity Scorecard. Check for slow drift. If scores are dropping, run one calibration cycle.
>
> **Every 6 months:** Re-read your Voice Profile. Has your voice evolved? New phrases? Shed old habits? Update to v2.0, v3.0. The profile is a living document, but changes are INTENTIONAL, never accidental.
>
> **When you encounter a new topic/niche:** Test the profile on that topic before producing a real script. If it wobbles, add topic-specific vocabulary constraints.
>
> **The goal:** Zero manual voice editing. The profile does the work. You focus on vision and craft. The AI handles execution. Your voice is locked.
