## 7. Permanent Layer 4 Prompt Template

*Once your Voice Profile is finalized, copy this block into your Layered Prompting Playbook as your default Layer 4 prompt. Use it unchanged for every script.*

**Layer 4 — Voice Overlay Prompt:**  
“Rewrite the following script using this Voice Profile:  

[PASTE YOUR PERMANENT VOICE PROFILE HERE]  

Maintain all pacing tokens, structural beats, and hook phrasing exactly as written. Do not alter the emotional arc of the script—only revoice the language to match the profile. The goal is to make the script sound like the person described in the profile, consistently across the entire piece.”
