## 7. Voice-DNA Integration Checklist

Use this after you’ve built and tested your Voice Profile to permanently wire it into your workflow.

- [ ] My Voice Profile v1.0 is saved in my Prompt Lab (with a clear filename, e.g., “Voice‑Profile‑v1.0”).  
- [ ] The profile covers all four dimensions with concrete, prompt‑ready language (no vague adjectives).  
- [ ] I’ve tested it on at least two different scripts (educational and personal) and the voice held across both.  
- [ ] I’ve replaced any ad‑hoc voice instructions in my Layer 4 prompt with the permanent Voice Overlay Prompt (4.3).  
- [ ] My Voice Profile is referenced in my Layered Prompting Workspace Template under Layer 4.  
- [ ] I’ve removed or archived old voice prompts to avoid confusion.  
- [ ] I’ve set a calendar reminder for 3 months from now: “Re‑test Voice Profile. Has my voice evolved? If so, run Calibration Loop again.”
