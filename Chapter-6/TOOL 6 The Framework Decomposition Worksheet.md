### The Decomposition Principle (tape this to your wall)

> **A framework name is shorthand for YOU. The AI gets the blueprint.**
>
> I never say "use [framework]."
> I say: "Beat 1: [emotional job]. Content: [requirement]. Mandatory: [specific field]."
> Beat by beat. Job by job. Field by field.
> That's the translation layer. That's the skill.
