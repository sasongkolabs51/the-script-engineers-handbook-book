## 7. Framework‑to‑Prompt Converter (Workflow Card)

*This is your step‑by‑step flow for upgrading Layer 1. Run through it each time you start a new script.*

**Step 1:** Choose your video topic and emotional goal.  
**Step 2:** Select the framework that best matches that goal (use the Selection Guide).  
**Step 3:** Open the corresponding Beat Script Builder (PAS, Hero’s Journey, or Hook‑Story‑Offer).  
**Step 4:** For each beat, fill in any customizations (your core insight, specific examples, callback details).  
**Step 5:** Copy the prompt snippets into a single prompt, in order.  
**Step 6:** Append your chosen Guardrail Stack (at least three).  
**Step 7:** Optionally, ask the AI to first expand your notes into full beat descriptions with all mandatory fields populated, then use that as the Layer 2 prompt.  
**Step 8:** Run Layer 2 (Structural Skeleton) with this engineered beat script.
