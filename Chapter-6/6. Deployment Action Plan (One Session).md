## 6. Deployment Action Plan (One Session)

- [ ] **Step 1 – Select and build your beat script.** Choose an upcoming video topic. Use the Framework Selection Worksheet and then the Beat Script Builder Template for your chosen framework. Write out or adapt the beat script in your own words.  
- [ ] **Step 2 – Assemble your guardrails.** From the Guardrail Stack Template, pick the three guardrails most important for this video. Copy them into your Layer 2 prompt document.  
- [ ] **Step 3 – Run the full chain.** Execute Layer 1 (ideation with framework) and then Layer 2 using Prompt 4.2 with your beat script and guardrails. Then run Layers 3 and 4 as usual.  
- [ ] **Step 4 – Narrative audit.** After the final script is ready, go through and label each beat in the margin. Check that the emotional arc matches the framework, that the final beat calls back to the first, and that no beats were collapsed. Rate the “narrative satisfaction” 1–10. If below 8, use the Troubleshooting Guide (Section 7) to pinpoint and fix the weak beat(s).
