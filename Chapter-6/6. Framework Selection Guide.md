## 6. Framework Selection Guide

*Match your video’s primary emotional goal to the framework most likely to deliver it.*

| **If your video’s emotional goal is…** | **Consider this framework first** |
|----------------------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| Transform a pain point into an actionable solution | PAS (Problem‑Agitation‑Solution) |
| Tell a personal journey of growth, failure, or discovery | Hero’s Journey (video‑condensed 7 beats) |
| Deliver a direct response, case study, or persuasive offer | Hook‑Story‑Offer |
| Challenge a belief and reframe a perspective | Contrarian Claim → Evidence Stack → Reframe (custom) |
| Share a reaction or analysis with emotional layers | Reaction‑Insight (see Chapter 12 vault) |
| Educate with deep‑dive logic and layered understanding | Explain‑Expand‑Apply (custom) |

*When in doubt, start with PAS—it’s the most versatile for online video.*
