## 5. Framework Integration into Layered Prompting (Updated Workflow)

Here’s how the Framework Fluency pillar slots into your existing Layered Prompting chain from Chapter 4.

**Layer 1 – Ideation (Expanded)**
- Choose topic, angle, emotional outcome, and **now select the framework** using the Framework Selection Worksheet.
- Optionally, use the Framework Beat Script Generator (Prompt 4.1) to get a first draft of the beat script. Review and personalise it, ensuring the mandatory fields fit your video.
- Store the final beat script in your workspace.

**Layer 2 – Structural Skeleton (Framework‑Powered)**
- Paste your beat script and the relevant Guardrail Stack into the Full Skeleton Prompt (4.2).
- Generate the skeleton. Review against the Handoff Criterion, now with an added check: *“Does each beat fulfil its emotional job and contain its mandatory field?”*
- If a beat fails, use the Beat Debugging Prompt (4.3) to fix only that section.

**Layers 3 & 4** – Pacing Injection and Voice Overlay proceed as usual, but now on a skeleton that already has an emotional arc, making pacing and voice work more intuitive.
