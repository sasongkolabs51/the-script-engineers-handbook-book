## TOOL 1: Framework Selection Guide

### *Match the framework to your video's emotional architecture. Choose before you build.*

---

### The Decision Matrix

| Your Video's Core Purpose | Best Framework | Why | Emotional Arc Shape |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Transform the viewer** — they have a problem, you have the fix | **PAS** (Problem-Agitation-Solution) | Builds tension through escalating pain, then releases it with a specific solution. The viewer feels the cost of inaction before receiving the answer. | Tension rises → peaks → releases → resolves |
| **Tell a transformation story** — you (or someone) went through a journey | **Hero's Journey** (7-beat condensed) | Creates identification through struggle, makes the lesson feel *earned* rather than preached. The viewer walks the path alongside the protagonist. | Stable → disrupted → struggle → breakthrough → return |
| **Convert or persuade** — you want the viewer to take a specific action | **Hook-Story-Offer** | Combines attention (hook), emotional proof (story), and a clear next step (offer). The viewer is primed emotionally before the ask. | Spike → immerse → insight → act |
| **Explain a counterintuitive idea** — challenge assumptions | **Contrarian Essay** (preview: Ch. 12) | Steelman → pivot → reframe → evidence → new model | Agreement → disruption → reconstruction |
| **Teach a process** — step-by-step how-to | **Problem-Process-Payoff** (preview: Ch. 12) | Clear pain → methodical walkthrough → transformed outcome | Pain → clarity → capability |
| **React / comment** — respond to a trend, news, or other creator | **Reaction-Insight-Application** (preview: Ch. 12) | Show the thing → extract the lesson → apply to viewer | Observation → extraction → relevance |

---

### The Quick Selector (30 seconds)

**1. What's the emotional journey I want the viewer to walk?**

☐ Pain → Discomfort → Desperation → Relief → Empowerment → **PAS**
☐ Normalcy → Disruption → Struggle → Discovery → Transformation → **Hero's Journey**
☐ Curiosity → Immersion → Insight → Action → **Hook-Story-Offer**

**2. What's my strongest material for this video?**

☐ A specific problem I've solved (with data/results) → **PAS**
☐ A personal story with a clear before/after → **Hero's Journey**
☐ A compelling case study + a clear offer/CTA → **Hook-Story-Offer**

**3. What's the video length?**

☐ Under 5 minutes → **Hook-Story-Offer** (tight, conversion-focused)
☐ 5–12 minutes → **PAS** (room for escalation layers)
☐ 10–20 minutes → **Hero's Journey** (space for the full arc)

---

### Framework Combination Plays

For longer or more complex videos, you can layer frameworks:

| Combination | When to Use | Structure |
|---|---|---|
| PAS + Hero's Journey | Transformation story with escalating stakes | Beats 1–3 of PAS (Problem + Agitation) become the "Ordinary World → Ordeal" of the Journey. The Solution becomes the "Reward → Return." |
| Hook-Story-Offer + PAS | Sales/conversion video with deep pain agitation | Hook (Ch. 5 archetype) → Story (PAS Beats 1–4 compressed) → Offer (PAS Beats 5–6 + CTA) |
| Hero's Journey + Hook-Story-Offer | Personal story that ends with a product/resource recommendation | Journey Beats 1–6 as the Story → Beat 7 becomes the Offer |
