**Instructions:** Fill in the top section, then circle the framework that aligns with your video’s purpose and desired viewer journey.

**Framework Options:**

| Framework | Best for… | Emotional Arc |
|-----------|-----------|---------------|
| **PAS (Problem‑Agitation‑Solution)** | Tutorials, how‑to’s, transformation content where you solve a pain point. | Recognition → rising discomfort → relief → empowerment |
| **Hero’s Journey (condensed 7‑beat)** | Personal stories, founder journeys, case studies with a clear before‑after. | Relatability → tension → rock bottom → breakthrough → universal lesson |
| **Hook‑Story‑Offer** | Direct‑response content, pitching an idea or product, short‑form persuasion. | Curiosity → emotional engagement → earned trust → action |
