## 8. Daily Framework Fluency Identity Anchor

Read aloud before your first script‑engineering session that uses a new framework.

> I am not just naming a framework—I am programming an emotional arc.  
> Every beat has a job, a content requirement, and a mandatory detail that makes it real.  
> I don’t ask the AI to “use PAS.” I give it a blueprint with concrete commands.  
> My guardrails keep the machine out of summary mode and inside the story.  
> The skeleton I build today will have a spine—not just paragraphs.  
> When the final beat echoes the first, I know the loop is closed and the viewer is satisfied.  
> Narrative structure is no longer a mystery I hope the AI guesses; it’s an architecture I dictate.
