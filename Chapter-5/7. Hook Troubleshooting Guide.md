## 7. Hook Troubleshooting Guide

| **Problem** | **Likely Cause** | **Fix** |
|-------------|------------------|---------|
| Hook feels generic, could apply to any video | Missing concrete detail (Specificity criterion) | Add to prompt: “Include a specific number, date, or location that makes this hook feel uniquely personal.” |
| Retention chain breaks after the hook (next sentence sounds like a generic intro) | Transition is summarizing rather than escalating | Add: “The sentence immediately after the hook must not summarize or explain the hook. It must deepen the mystery, raise the stakes, or introduce a surprising twist.” |
| Hook feels clickbaity or manipulative | Contrarian Claim or Identity Challenge pushed too hard without authentic grounding | Add: “The hook must feel like a genuine insight, not a provocation. Back the claim with the specific experience or evidence you’ll reveal in the video.” |
| AI ignores the retention mechanism you requested | Description of mechanism too abstract | Add an explicit example: e.g., “An Open Loop Extension looks like this: [paste a short example from your niche].” |
| Hooks all sound the same across videos | Over‑relying on one archetype | Use the Archetype Selection Guide above to vary based on emotional goal. Run a Prompt Lab experiment testing two different archetypes for the same video. |
