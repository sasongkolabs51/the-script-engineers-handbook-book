## 8. Daily Hook Craft Identity Anchor

Read aloud before any hook‑generating session.

> The first fifteen seconds are not an introduction—they are a psychological doorway.  
> I don’t “make it engaging.” I select an archetype: Curiosity, Contrarian, Pain, Promise, Identity, Open Loop.  
> I chain attention directly into retention—no polite filler, no topic sentences.  
> I score my hooks against Specificity, Emotion, Curiosity, Authenticity, and Chain.  
> If it’s below a four, I don’t settle; I debug.  
> The hook is the gatekeeper of everything I built.  
> Today, I engineer the moment that makes the scroll stop.
