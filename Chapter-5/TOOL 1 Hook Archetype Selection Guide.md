## TOOL 1: Hook Archetype Selection Guide

### *Before you write a single word, choose your mechanism. Match the archetype to your video's emotional goal.*

---

### The Decision Matrix

| Your Video's Primary Emotional Goal | Best Archetype | Why It Works |
|---|---|---|
| Make them feel **curious** — they NEED to know the answer | **Curiosity Gap** | Incomplete information creates psychological discomfort. The brain must close the gap. |
| Make them feel **challenged** — their assumptions are wrong | **Contrarian Claim** | Cognitive dissonance grabs processing power. The brain allocates extra attention to conflicting info. |
| Make them feel **seen** — "that's exactly my life" | **Relatable Pain** | Accurate self-description creates instant bonding. The viewer thinks "this person GETS me." |
| Make them feel **hopeful** — "I can actually do this" | **Specific Promise** | Clear reward + low perceived cost = easy mental "yes." The viewer calculates ROI instantly. |
| Make them feel **unsettled** — "wait, am I doing this wrong?" | **Identity Challenge** | Threatening a positive self-concept creates urgent tension. The viewer must resolve the contradiction. |
| Make them feel **pulled forward** — "what happens next?" | **Open Loop** | Narrative incompleteness is almost physically intrusive. The brain craves closure. |

---

### The Quick Selector (30 seconds)

Answer these three questions. Your answers point to the archetype.

**1. What's the FIRST emotion I want the viewer to feel in second one?**

☐ Surprise / "Wait, what?" → Curiosity Gap or Contrarian Claim
☐ Recognition / "That's me" → Relatable Pain
☐ Desire / "I want that outcome" → Specific Promise
☐ Discomfort / "Am I the problem?" → Identity Challenge
☐ Narrative pull / "What happens next?" → Open Loop

**2. What's my strongest personal asset for this topic?**

☐ A surprising number or data point I discovered → Curiosity Gap
☐ A genuinely contrarian opinion I've earned through experience → Contrarian Claim
☐ A specific, vivid failure or frustration I lived through → Relatable Pain
☐ A concrete, measurable result I achieved → Specific Promise
☐ A behavior I used to do that contradicted my values → Identity Challenge
☐ A story with a natural cliffhanger moment → Open Loop

**3. What's the retention risk for this video?**

☐ Viewers might think "I already know this" → Contrarian Claim (disrupts the assumption)
☐ Viewers might not feel the topic is about them → Relatable Pain or Identity Challenge
☐ Viewers might not see the value of watching → Specific Promise
☐ Viewers might feel no urgency → Curiosity Gap or Open Loop

---

### The Combination Play

You don't have to pick just one. The strongest hooks often layer two archetypes:

| Primary + Secondary | Example |
|---|---|
| Relatable Pain + Open Loop | "You know that feeling when [pain]... Well, last Tuesday, that feeling led me to [cliffhanger]." |
| Curiosity Gap + Identity Challenge | "Here's a number that should embarrass every [identity]... [surprising stat]. And if you're thinking 'that's not me,' keep watching." |
| Contrarian Claim + Specific Promise | "[Common belief] is wrong. And once you see why, you'll [specific outcome] by the end of this video." |
| Specific Promise + Open Loop | "By the end of this video, you'll [specific outcome]. But the method I used? It started with [unresolved story beat]." |
