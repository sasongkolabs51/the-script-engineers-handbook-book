## 7. Hook Deployment Action Plan (One Session)

- [ ] **Step 1 – Choose your video.** Fill out the Hook Archetype Selection Worksheet to pick your archetype and retention mechanism.  
- [ ] **Step 2 – Generate hooks.** Use the Hook Generation Machine prompt (5.1) to get three versions.  
- [ ] **Step 3 – Score and pick.** Run each hook through the Scroll‑Stop Scorecard. Select the one that scores 4 or 5. If none do, use the Hook Debugging Prompt to improve the best one.  
- [ ] **Step 4 – Integrate into your workflow.** Paste the winning hook into the Hook‑to‑Skeleton Integration prompt along with your beat sheet. Execute and continue through Layers 2–4.  
- [ ] **Step 5 – Reflect.** After publishing, compare the retention graph of this video to your last one without engineered hooks. Note the drop‑off at 30 seconds.
