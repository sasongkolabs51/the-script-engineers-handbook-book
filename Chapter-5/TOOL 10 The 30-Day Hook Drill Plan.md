## TOOL 10: The 30-Day Hook Drill Plan

### *Build fluency across all six archetypes. One hook per day. Twenty minutes. No exceptions.*

---

### The Daily Drill (20 minutes)

Every day for 30 days:

1. **Pick a topic** from your content backlog (or invent one). (2 min)
2. **Select an archetype** (rotate through all six — see schedule below). (1 min)
3. **Write the Hook Generation Machine prompt** (Tool 6) for that topic + archetype. (3 min)
4. **Generate three hooks.** (2 min)
5. **Score all three** with the Scroll-Stop Scorecard (Tool 4). (5 min)
6. **Pick the winner.** Note why it won. (2 min)
7. **Write one sentence:** "The principle I confirmed today: ___________" (2 min)
8. **Discard the output.** You're not saving these. You're building instinct. (0 min)
9. **Log it** in the table below. (3 min)
