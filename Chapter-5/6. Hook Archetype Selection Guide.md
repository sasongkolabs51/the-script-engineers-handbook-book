## 6. Hook Archetype Selection Guide

*Match the emotional goal of your video to the hook archetype that naturally amplifies it.*

| **If your video’s primary emotional goal is…** | **Start with this archetype first** |
|------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------|
| To make viewers feel seen, understood, or validated | Relatable Pain |
| To challenge a common assumption and make viewers rethink their approach | Contrarian Claim |
| To create intrigue and make viewers feel they’re about to learn something they can’t find anywhere else | Curiosity Gap |
| To give viewers a clear, tangible outcome they desperately want | Specific Promise |
| To create an “I need to fix this” tension by pointing out a contradiction between their identity and their actions | Identity Challenge |
| To create emotional investment through unfinished narrative | Open Loop |

*You can layer archetypes, but always lead with the one that matches the primary emotional job of the video.*
