### Scoring

| Total Score | Verdict | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **5/5 (25 points)** | Exceptional. Record it. | Proceed to Layer 2. |
| **4/5 (20–24 points)** | Strong. Ship it. | Proceed to Layer 2. Note the weak criterion for future refinement. |
| **3/5 (15–19 points)** | Not yet. Fixable. | Identify the failing criterion. Adjust the prompt for that specific element. Regenerate. Re-score. |
| **2/5 or below (≤14 points)** | Weak. Rethink. | The archetype may be wrong for this topic. Go back to Tool 1. Try a different archetype. Or the specificity is too low — add a concrete detail from your own experience. |

---

### The 3-Second Test (Bonus)

Read the hook aloud. Then stop. Wait three seconds. Ask yourself:

> "If I were scrolling through YouTube at 11 p.m., half-asleep, thumb moving on autopilot — would this sentence make my thumb STOP?"

☐ Yes, immediately.
☐ Maybe. I'd slow down but might keep scrolling.
☐ No. I'd scroll past.

If the answer isn't an immediate "yes," the hook isn't ready. Specificity and emotional spike are usually the culprits.
