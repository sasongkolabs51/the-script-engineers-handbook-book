## 3. Hook‑Loop Prompt Builder (Fill‑in Template)

**Purpose:** Turn your chosen archetype into a full, chained opening that grabs attention and immediately locks retention. This prompt becomes the opening section of your script generation.

**Step 1 – Select the Hook Archetype.** Choose from the six archetypes above and copy its prompt instruction snippet.  
*My hook instruction:* ________________________________________________

**Step 2 – Choose a Retention Mechanism.** Pick one that extends the hook’s momentum.

| Mechanism | What It Does | Prompt Addition |
|-----------|--------------|-----------------|
| **Open Loop Extension** | Deepens the initial mystery without solving it. | “Immediately follow the hook with 1–2 sentences that intensify the mystery. Add a specific, tantalizing detail that makes the unanswered question even more urgent. Do not give the answer.” |
| **Pattern Interrupt** | Subverts an expectation set by the hook. | “Immediately follow the hook with a sentence that contradicts what the viewer would naturally predict next. Reveal that the real reason/outcome is not what they think—it’s something stranger/deeper/more surprising.” |
| **Stake Escalation** | Raises the personal or professional stakes introduced by the hook. | “Immediately follow the hook by revealing a broader or more serious consequence that the viewer might not have considered. Connect the specific hook to a larger threat or opportunity in the viewer’s life.” |

**Step 3 – Combine into a single prompt command.**

> “Open this script with a [Hook Archetype] hook: [paste archetype instruction]. Immediately follow with a [Retention Mechanism]: [paste mechanism instruction]. Do not reveal the core answer or resolution until the designated section of the video.”

**Step 4 – Optional: Request Multiple Versions.** Add: “Generate three distinct versions of this opening, varying the specific example or wording while maintaining the same psychological structure.”
