## 6. Expanded Layer 1 Ideation Template (With Hook Architecture)

**Purpose:** Upgrade your Layered Prompting Layer 1 from Chapter 4 to include the hook architecture as a mandatory field.

### LAYER 1 — IDEATION (with Hook Spec)

```
Topic: [your topic]
Angle: [your specific, contrarian, or personal take]
Target audience: [who you’re speaking to]
Emotional outcome: [what they should feel by the end]
Structure: [chosen narrative framework]

HOOK ARCHITECTURE:
- Primary archetype: [choose one from the six]
- Retention mechanism: [Open Loop Extension / Pattern Interrupt / Stake Escalation]
- Hook must pass the Scroll‑Stop Scorecard (≥4/5) before proceeding.

Generate a beat sheet with 5–7 distinct beats. Each beat is one sentence describing the content and purpose of that section. Do not write prose yet—only the beat sheet.

(After the beat sheet, the hook will be generated separately using the Hook Generation Machine prompt, or you can integrate it here if you prefer to generate the hook and beat sheet in one go by adding: “Additionally, generate the opening hook according to the architecture above.”)
```
