## 10. Retention Chain Visual Map (Mental Model)

*Picture your opening as a chain of three links. If any link breaks, the viewer scrolls away.*

**Link 1 — The Hook (0–3 seconds)**  
A specific, emotionally charged sentence that forces a reaction: “Wait, what?” or “That’s so true!” or “No way.”

**Link 2 — The Retention Mechanism (3–10 seconds)**  
The sentence(s) that follow the hook, designed to:  
- Deepen the curiosity (Open Loop Extension)  
- Subvert an expectation (Pattern Interrupt)  
- Raise the personal stakes (Stake Escalation)

**Link 3 — The Handoff (10–15 seconds)**  
A smooth bridge into the body of the video that *keeps the loop open* and signals that the payoff is coming, without summarizing or “introducing” the topic generically.

*Whenever retention drops early, diagnose which link broke and fix only that link. Don’t rewrite the whole opening.*
