## TOOL 6: The Hook Generation Machine (All-in-One Prompt)

### *Your reusable Layer 1 sub-prompt. Generates three engineered hooks + retention locks in one shot. Plug into your Layered Prompting workflow.*

---

```
═══ HOOK GENERATION BRIEF ═══

TOPIC: [your specific topic]
TARGET AUDIENCE: [age, knowledge level, primary struggle, what
they've already tried]
EMOTIONAL GOAL OF VIDEO: [what the viewer should feel by the end]
MY PERSONAL ANGLE: [why MY take on this is different — my
experience, my data, my story]
MY VOICE: [2-sentence voice description from your Voice DNA Profile]

═══ ARCHETYPE SELECTION ═══

Generate hooks using the following archetypes (one version each):
1. [Primary archetype: e.g., Curiosity Gap]
2. [Secondary archetype: e.g., Relatable Pain]
3. [Wildcard: e.g., Open Loop]

═══ RETENTION MECHANISM ═══

For each hook, chain the following retention mechanism:
- Hook 1: [e.g., Pattern Interrupt — the next sentence breaks
  the expectation the hook creates]
- Hook 2: [e.g., Open Loop Extension — deepen the mystery
  without solving it]
- Hook 3: [e.g., Stake Escalation — raise from personal to
  universal]

═══ STRUCTURAL REQUIREMENTS ═══

Each hook + retention lock must:
- Be 60–80 words total (4–6 sentences)
- Contain at least ONE specific concrete detail (number, name,
  date, sensory moment)
- Trigger a nameable emotion in the first 3 seconds
- Flow as one continuous thought (no "Anyway, in this video...")
- End with a forward-pointing sentence that hands off to the body
- NOT greet the audience
- NOT state the topic directly
- NOT summarize the video content
- NOT use: "Hey everyone," "Welcome back," "In today's video,"
  "Let's dive in," "Have you ever wondered"

═══ OUTPUT FORMAT ═══

For each of the three hooks, provide:
1. The hook + retention lock text (60–80 words)
2. Archetype used
3. Retention mechanism used
4. The implicit question the viewer now needs answered
5. The specific emotion triggered

Then recommend which of the three is strongest for this specific
topic and audience, and explain why in one sentence.
```

---

### How to Use This in Your Layered Prompting Workflow

1. Run this prompt as a **sub-step of Layer 1** (after your beat sheet is approved).
2. Evaluate the three outputs with the Scroll-Stop Scorecard (Tool 4).
3. Select the winner.
4. Paste the winning hook into your **Layer 2 prompt** as the opening (see Tool 8).
5. Proceed with the standard Layered Prompting chain.
