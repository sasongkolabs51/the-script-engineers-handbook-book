## 11. One‑Minute Hook Audit (Pre‑Record Check)

*Before you hit record, read your final opening aloud and answer these three questions in under a minute:*

1. **Does this opening sound like a person, or like a presenter?** (If it sounds like a presenter, your voice overlay may have been too generic. Re‑voice with more specific characteristics.)  
2. **If I heard this on my feed, would I stop scrolling?** (If you’re not sure, go back and strengthen the Specificity and Emotional Spike.)  
3. **Is there a clear unanswered question that carries past the first 15 seconds?** (If the answer arrives too quickly, extend the open loop.)
