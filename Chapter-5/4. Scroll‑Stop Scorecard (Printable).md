## 4. Scroll‑Stop Scorecard (Printable)

**Purpose:** Evaluate any AI‑generated hook in 30 seconds. A score of 4 or 5 out of 5 is needed to proceed to the structural skeleton; otherwise, tweak the prompt at the failing criteria and regenerate.

**Instructions:** Read the hook aloud. For each criterion, check “Yes” if it clearly meets the description. Count your Yes marks.

- [ ] **Specificity** – Does the hook contain a concrete detail (number, date, name, sensory detail) that couldn’t apply to any other video on the topic?  
- [ ] **Emotional Spike** – Can you name a distinct emotion the hook triggers in you (curiosity, surprise, recognition, indignation, amusement)?  
- [ ] **Curiosity Gap Magnitude** – After hearing the hook, do you have a specific, urgent question you need answered? (Write it here: ________________________)  
- [ ] **Niche‑Specific Authenticity** – Does this hook sound like something *only you* would say, given your voice and perspective? Or could it sit on any generic channel?  
- [ ] **Retention Chain Presence** – Does the hook flow naturally into a retention mechanism (open loop, pattern interrupt, stake escalation) without a jarring “in this video we’ll explore” transition?

**Total Yes marks:** ____ / 5

- **5/5** – Excellent. Proceed.  
- **4/5** – Strong. Consider a small tweak to the weakest point before finalising.  
- **3 or below** – Return to the prompt, read the failing criteria, and add explicit instructions to address them. Regenerate.
