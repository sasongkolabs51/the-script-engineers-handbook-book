### VOICE LAB (Odd Weeks)

**Purpose:** Understand your voice's range. How far can you push each dimension before it breaks? Where are the elastic limits?

**This week's experiment:**

| Dimension | Modification | What I'm Testing |
|---|---|---|
| ☐ Sentence Architecture | e.g., "Double my fragment frequency" or "Remove all fragments" | How much do fragments contribute to my voice identity? |
| ☐ Humor Density | e.g., "Increase to 70% of paragraphs" or "Remove all humor" | Is my voice still recognizable without humor? With double humor? |
| ☐ Emotional Temperature | e.g., "Shift to maximum warmth" or "Shift to clinical detachment" | Where does my voice stop sounding like me? |
| ☐ Colloquial Fingerprint | e.g., "Replace all signature phrases with formal equivalents" | How much do my pet phrases matter to identity? |

**The Experiment Prompt:**

```
Take the following script section (200 words) and rewrite it with
this voice modification:

[PASTE A 200-WORD SECTION FROM A RECENT SCRIPT]

MODIFICATION: [e.g., "Increase humor density to 70%. Every
paragraph must contain a joke or wry observation. Maintain my
humor TYPE (dry, self-deprecating) but triple the frequency."]

All other voice dimensions remain at baseline.

After the rewrite, add a "Voice Stress Test" note:
- Does this still sound like me? (Yes / Partially / No)
- At what point did it stop sounding like me? (Which sentence?)
- What does this reveal about which dimension is most essential
  to my voice identity?
```

### PACING LAB (Even Weeks)

**Purpose:** Train your ear for rhythm. Feel how different pacing signatures change the emotional impact of the same words.

**This week's experiment:**

Take the SAME 200-word script section and score it THREE different ways:

**Version A: Rapid and Punchy**

```
Rewrite this section with [Fast energy] throughout. Short sentences.
Fragments. [Pause 0.5s] only before the single biggest reveal.
[Build speed] leading into the climax. No [Slow down]. No [Pause 3s].
Maximum momentum.
```

**Version B: Slow and Reflective**

```
Rewrite this section with [Slow down] throughout. Longer sentences.
[Pause 1.5s] after every key statement. [Whisper] for the most
vulnerable line. [Pause 3s] at the emotional peak. No [Fast energy].
No [Hard cut]. Maximum weight.
```

**Version C: Hybrid (Shifts Mid-Section)**

```
Rewrite this section with a pacing SHIFT: the first half uses
[Fast energy] and [Build speed]. At the midpoint, insert [Pause 2s]
and shift to [Slow down] for the second half. The contrast IS
the pacing. The shift should feel like a door opening.
```

**The Test:** Record yourself reading all three versions. Listen back.
