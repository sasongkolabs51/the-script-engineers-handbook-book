## 7. Lifelong Mastery Rhythm Reference Card

*Keep this on your wall or in your Prompt Lab. It’s the heartbeat of your new creative identity.*

| **Rhythm** | **Action** | **Time Investment** |
|------------|------------|---------------------|
| **Daily** | Update Engineering Log after each script. | ~5 minutes |
| **Weekly** | Complete Prompt Practice (structural drill, voice/pacing lab, reflection). | 60 minutes |
| **Monthly** | Run the Skill Audit. Review Breakthrough Tracker. Set one deliberate experiment. | 15–20 minutes |
| **Quarterly** | Review and update Creator Manifesto. Run Voice Calibration Loop. Review Failure Autopsies for patterns. | 1 hour |

**Core mantra:** *“I do not write scripts. I engineer them.”*
