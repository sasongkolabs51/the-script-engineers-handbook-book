## 3. Reset Protocol Step‑by‑Step Checklist

*When output degrades unexpectedly, follow this sequence. It prevents panic and points you to the exact fix.*

☐ **Step 1: Isolate the Layer.**  
Run each layer of your chain independently on a simple test topic.  
☐ Ideation (Layer 1): Does the beat sheet still look coherent?  
☐ Structural Skeleton (Layer 2): Does it follow the framework when prompted alone?  
☐ Pacing Injection (Layer 3): Does it work on a clean skeleton?  
☐ Voice Overlay (Layer 4): Does it sound right on a neutral script?  

☐ **Step 2: Compare to a Known‑Good Snapshot.**  
Open your Engineering Log and find an example of strong output from the layer that seems off. What specific quality is missing?  

☐ **Step 3: Adjust the Affected Layer Only.**  
☐ Voice underperforming? → Re‑run the Voice Calibration Loop (Chapter 7) with fresh samples.  
☐ Pacing tokens not respected? → Tighten the pacing injection prompt with explicit placement examples.  
☐ Structure drifting? → Add or strengthen guardrail prompts (Chapter 6).  

☐ **Step 4: Document the Reset.**  
Record the trigger, the fix, and any proactive measures in the Failure Autopsy section of your Engineering Log.

**Emotional check:** Remind yourself that a system that needs occasional recalibration is normal. Your identity is not threatened; it’s demonstrated by your ability to diagnose and repair.
