## 7. AI Prompt Templates for Reflection

Use these prompts with your AI co‑creator to help you populate your logs or work through a reset.

### 7.1 Breakthrough Tracker Assistant

```
I just had an insight while practicing script engineering. I’ll describe it in my own words, and I want you to help me articulate it clearly and suggest how I can apply it.

My raw thought: “[e.g., My storytelling videos feel rushed because I’m using the wrong pacing template.]”

Please:
1. Rephrase this insight as a clear, memorable principle.
2. Suggest one concrete way I could test or apply this principle in my next script.
3. Note any related pitfalls I should watch out for.
```

### 7.2 Failure Autopsy Assistant

```
One of my videos underperformed. Here’s what I know:
- Topic and niche: [describe]
- Hook used: [describe]
- Framework used: [describe]
- The symptom: [e.g., retention dropped sharply after 2 minutes, or very low subscribe conversion]
- My hunch about the cause: [optional]

Based on typical script‑engineering failure patterns, can you suggest:
1. Which layer (Hook, Structure, Voice, Pacing, Payoff, Niche Adaptation) is most likely at fault?
2. A diagnostic test I could run to confirm?
3. A specific fix I could apply to that layer’s prompt for next time?
```

### 7.3 Reset Protocol Guide

```
I think my script engineering chain is producing degraded output, but I’m not sure why. Here’s a summary of what’s happening:
[Describe symptoms]

Please walk me through the Reset Protocol:
1. Help me isolate which layer might be failing by suggesting small, independent tests for each layer.
2. Remind me what to compare against (what does a “known‑good” version look like for that layer?).
3. Suggest potential adjustments I could make to that layer’s prompt.
4. End with a brief motivational note that reinforces the Script Engineer identity—systems need recalibration, and I’m capable of doing it.
```
