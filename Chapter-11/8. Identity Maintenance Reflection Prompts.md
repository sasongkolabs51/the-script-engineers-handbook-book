## 8. Identity Maintenance Reflection Prompts

*Use these whenever you feel the pull of old habits—template hoarding, guilt about AI, or doubt about the system.*

- **When I see a “magic prompt” shared online:** Instead of saving it, I will extract the underlying principle and test it in my Prompt Lab within 24 hours. (Chapter 2 habit)  
- **When my output feels off and I’m tempted to start over:** I will run the Reset Protocol—isolate the layer, compare to a known‑good snapshot, and adjust only what’s broken.  
- **When I feel guilty about using AI:** I will return to my Creator Manifesto and remind myself that my Visionary and Craftsman layers are irreplaceable, and the AI operates only in the Engineer layer. (Chapter 3)  
- **When I hit a plateau and think “I’m not making progress”:** I will open my Engineering Log and look at the arc—from Chapter 1 to now. The evidence is there.
