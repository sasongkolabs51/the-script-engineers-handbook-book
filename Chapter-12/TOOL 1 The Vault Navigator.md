### How to Use the Vault

The Vault is not a collection you browse when you're stuck. It's a curriculum you study to deepen your engineering language. Every template in it was built using the decomposition method from Chapter 6. Every beat has an emotional job. Every guardrail prevents a specific failure mode. When you open a template, you're not looking for a prompt to copy—you're reading the *engineering decisions* that produced it.

**The Vault Protocol:**

1. **Before using a template:** Read the beat script. Identify WHY each beat exists. What emotional job does it serve? What would break if you removed it?
2. **After using a template:** Note what worked and what didn't. Feed the data into the Evolution Loop (Tool 2).
3. **Monthly:** Pick one template you've never used. Reverse-engineer it (Tool 5). Remix it. Swap the hook. Change the pacing. Break it and rebuild it. This is how you learn the language it's written in.
4. **Quarterly:** Build one new template from scratch (Tool 4). Add it to the Vault with your own tags.

### The Tag System

Tag each template so you can find the right structure for any video:

**Structure tags:** ☐ PAS ☐ Hero's Journey ☐ HSO ☐ Reaction-Insight ☐ Contrarian Essay ☐ Process-Breakdown ☐ Before-After-Bridge ☐ Hybrid: ___

**Niche tags:** ☐ Educational ☐ Review ☐ Story/Vlog ☐ Reaction ☐ Opinion ☐ Tutorial ☐ Case Study ☐ Hybrid: ___

**Emotional intensity:** ☐ Low (calm, reflective) ☐ Medium (engaged, curious) ☐ High (urgent, volatile) ☐ Oscillating (swings between registers)

**Length fit:** ☐ Short-form (<3 min) ☐ Medium (3–10 min) ☐ Long-form (10–20 min) ☐ Deep-dive (20+ min)

**Platform fit:** ☐ YouTube ☐ TikTok ☐ Reels ☐ LinkedIn ☐ Podcast ☐ Live ☐ Multi-platform

### The Vault as Curriculum: Monthly Study Plan

| Month | Template to Study | What to Reverse-Engineer | What to Remix |
|---|---|---|---|
| Month 1 | [Pick one you've used most] | Why does it work? Which beats are load-bearing? | Swap the hook archetype. How does the arc shift? |
| Month 2 | [Pick one you've NEVER used] | What emotional job does each beat serve? | Apply it to your most common topic. Does it fit? |
| Month 3 | [Pick one from a different niche] | How does the pacing signature differ from yours? | Adapt it to your niche. What changes? What stays? |
| Month 4 | [Pick one with a hybrid structure] | How do two frameworks combine? Where's the seam? | Build your own hybrid. Two frameworks, one script. |
| Month 5 | [Pick your weakest template] | Where does it consistently underperform? | Rebuild the weakest beat. Test. Compare. |
| Month 6 | [Build a new one from scratch — Tool 4] | N/A — you're creating, not studying. | Add to Vault. Tag it. Use it. |
