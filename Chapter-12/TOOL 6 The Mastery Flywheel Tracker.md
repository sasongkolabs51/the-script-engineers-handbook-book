### The Flywheel

```
        CREATE (publish scripts)
            │
            ▼
        COLLECT (data, insights, failures)
            │
            ▼
        DIAGNOSE (what worked, what didn't)
            │
            ▼
        REFINE (adjust templates, voice, pacing)
            │
            ▼
        PRACTICE (weekly drills, experiments)
            │
            ▼
        CREATE (better scripts, new formats)
            │
            ▼
        ... (the wheel turns)
```
