## 6. Lifetime Rhythm Checklist

A one‑page reference for the daily, weekly, monthly, and quarterly habits that keep the flywheel turning. Tick off items as you complete them. Keep this visible.

### Daily (2–5 min)
- [ ] After each script, update the Chain Archive in your Engineering Log (Chapter 11).
- [ ] If a “Breakthrough” insight occurs, capture it immediately in the Breakthrough Tracker.
- [ ] Spend 1 minute scanning today’s scheduled posts for any formatting or voice glitches.

### Weekly (60 min)
- [ ] Complete the Weekly Practice Ritual (Chapter 11): structural drill, voice/pacing variation lab, reflection.
- [ ] Fill out one Template Evolution Loop entry for a recent video (Section 3).
- [ ] Add or update at least one row in the Vault Navigator.

### Monthly (20 min)
- [ ] Complete the Monthly Skill Audit (Chapter 11): what improved, what was inconsistent, what experiment to run next.
- [ ] Review the Breakthrough Tracker. Choose one insight to deepen in next month’s practice.
- [ ] Run one “Remix Experiment” on a vault template using the Reverse‑Engineering Worksheet (Section 2).

### Quarterly (60 min)
- [ ] Re‑read your Creator Manifesto (Chapter 3). Update if your voice or values have shifted.
- [ ] Run the full Voice Calibration Loop (Chapter 7) with fresh script samples.
- [ ] Review all Template Evolution Loop entries for the quarter. Identify any template that needs a major redesign—or a new one to build.
- [ ] Run at least one Build-Your-Own-Template workshop (Section 5) for a new format, platform, or hybrid need.
- [ ] Review your Platform Adaptation Blueprints and adjust based on recent performance data or platform changes.
