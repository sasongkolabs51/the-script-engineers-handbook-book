**Simplified workflow for each video:**
- **Collect:** After 1 week, note the key metrics and any retention curve drop‑offs.  
- **Diagnose:** Match the drop‑off point to a beat in your template. (Early drop → hook; middle drop → agitation/pacing; late drop → payoff/CTA.)  
- **Adjust:** Modify the prompt for that specific beat. Make one change at a time.  
- **Retest:** Apply the new version to the next video on a similar topic and compare.

**Example entry:**  
Cycle 1: Video “Why I Quit Productivity Apps” — retention dropped at 1:30, right at the transition from hook to story. Diagnosis: hook promised a personal confession but the skeleton started with dry context. Adjustment: added guardrail to Layer 2: “Within the first 30 seconds after the hook, you must deliver the specific personal confession the hook promised.” v1.1. Next video: retention held through the transition. Improved.
