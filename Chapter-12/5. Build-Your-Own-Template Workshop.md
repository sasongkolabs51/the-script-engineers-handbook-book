## 5. Build-Your-Own-Template Workshop

**Purpose:** A repeatable methodology for inventing new frameworks from scratch. Use this whenever you encounter a new format, a trending structure, or a creative need that no existing template satisfies.

### Step 1 – Study the Model
Find 2–3 examples of the video type you want to reverse‑engineer. They can be your own past successes or another creator’s signature format. Watch each with a notebook.
