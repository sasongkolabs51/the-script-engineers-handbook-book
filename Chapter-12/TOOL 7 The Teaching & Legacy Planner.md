## TOOL 7: The Teaching & Legacy Planner

### *Share the system. Solidify your mastery. Build the ripple that outlasts any single video.*

---

### Why Teach?

Teaching is not altruism. It's the deepest form of learning. When you explain the Principle Extraction Protocol to a friend, you understand it more deeply than when you just use it. When you break down a framework for a community post, you see its structure more clearly than when you just prompt it. Every act of teaching strengthens the system in you.

---

### The Teaching Ladder

| Level | Action | Frequency | What It Strengthens |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1. Explain** | Answer a friend's question about your process. In your own words. No jargon. | As it arises | Clarity of understanding |
| **2. Demonstrate** | Record a behind-the-scenes of your Prompt Lab. Show the chain in action. | Monthly | Process fluency |
| **3. Break Down** | Write a thread/post dissecting ONE principle (e.g., "Why 'make it engaging' fails"). | Bi-weekly | Principle articulation |
| **4. Mentor** | Walk one person through building their Voice Profile or first beat script. | Quarterly | Systematic thinking |
| **5. Create** | Build a template pack, a mini-course, or a workshop for a specific sub-niche. | As inspired | Generative mastery |
