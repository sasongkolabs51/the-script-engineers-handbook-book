## 1. Vault Navigator Template

**Purpose:** Transform your collection of templates into a searchable, learnable curriculum. Use this index to tag every template in your library by its structural DNA, emotional register, niche compatibility, and the engineering principle it best teaches.

**Instructions:** Create a table in your Prompt Lab or a dedicated document. Each time you add or build a template, fill out a row. The “Principle to Extract” column is the most important—it forces you to see the template as a teaching tool, not just a tool.

**How to use this as a curriculum:**
- Pick a template you’ve never used. Before running it, open the Reverse‑Engineering Worksheet (Section 2) and dissect its beats, emotional jobs, and guardrails.  
- Then run it on a familiar topic and observe what the structure *does* to the content.  
- Finally, remix it: swap the hook archetype, change the pacing signature, or move a guardrail. This deliberate tinkering builds your architectural intuition far faster than passive use.
