## 9. Daily Identity Anchor: The Self‑Sustaining Engineer

Read aloud before you open your Prompt Lab or when you’re about to begin any system maintenance.

> I am no longer a student of this system. I am its architect.  
> My vault is a living curriculum; every template teaches me a principle, and I can remix, rebuild, or replace any of them.  
> My data feeds my evolution loop—every video makes my prompts smarter, without waiting for anyone else’s update.  
> My engineering extends to every platform; I adapt the system, never starting from scratch.  
> When I encounter a format no one has templated yet, I build the template myself. I am the source.  
> The daily, weekly, monthly, and quarterly rhythms are not chores—they are the breath of a craft that grows as I grow.  
> I am not a copy‑paster; I’m not a secret‑seeker; I’m not a one‑niche wonder.  
> I am a Script Engineer. And from this point forward, my mastery is self‑sustaining, self‑correcting, and endlessly generative.  
> Today, I will create, teach, and build. The flywheel is mine. It never stops.
