## 7. Quarterly Evolution Audit Checklist

*Once per quarter, run through this checklist to keep your system aligned and evolving.*

☐ **Creator Manifesto Review**  
☐ Re‑read the manifesto aloud. Does it still resonate?  
☐ Update any sections that no longer reflect your voice or values.  

☐ **Voice Calibration Loop**  
☐ Gather three recent script samples.  
☐ Run the extraction prompt on each.  
☐ Compare to your permanent Voice Profile. Any drift?  
☐ Adjust profile dimensions if needed.  

☐ **Template Evolution Loop Review**  
☐ Review your Evolution Loop Tracker for the past quarter.  
☐ Which templates improved the most? Which plateaued?  
☐ For plateaued templates, schedule a structural drill in your weekly practice.  

☐ **Failure Autopsy Pattern Check**  
☐ Review the Failure Autopsy section of your Engineering Log.  
☐ Any recurring layer failures? (e.g., hook repeatedly underperforming)  
☐ Plan one deliberate experiment to address the pattern next month.  

☐ **Platform & Niche Expansion**  
☐ Are there any new platforms or niches you want to adapt your system to?  
☐ If yes, build a Platform Signature or Niche Adaptation Blueprint column.
