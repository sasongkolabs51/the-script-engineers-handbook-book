## 4. CTA & Payoff Generator Prompt (Fill‑in Template)

**Purpose:** A layered, reusable prompt that produces three tailored CTA variants for any script. It integrates emotional resolution, session flow, voice, and pacing.

```
You are my Payoff Architect. Below is the hook and the final content beat of my script, plus my Voice Profile and niche. I need you to craft three complete payoff endings, each 20‑30 seconds when spoken.

HOOK (opening):
[PASTE YOUR HOOK]

FINAL CONTENT BEAT (the last section before the payoff):
[PASTE THE LAST PARAGRAPH OR TWO OF YOUR SCRIPT, e.g., the solution expansion or return beat]

VOICE PROFILE:
[PASTE YOUR VOICE PROFILE OR KEY ELEMENTS]

NICHE: [Educational / Reaction / Story‑Driven / Review / Other: _______]

PRIMARY CTA TRIGGERS (choose 1‑2 for the AI to use across variants):
[Identity Reinforcement / Curiosity Bridge / Social Proof Nudge / Empowered Utility / Emotional Debt Repayment]

PACING INSTRUCTIONS FOR ENDINGS (your niche’s payoff signature):
[PASTE YOUR PACING SIGNATURE FOR ENDINGS, e.g., “Slow down, use [Pause 1.5s] before the CTA, [Fade out energy] on the final line.”]

TASK:
1. Write a short Emotional Resolution Arc (Emotional Landing → Reflective Expansion → Forward Gaze) that connects the final content beat to the CTA. Ensure it provides a satisfying exhale before any ask.
2. Then, generate THREE distinct CTA variants, each using a different combination of the triggers above or a slightly different emotional tone, while keeping my voice and pacing intact. All three must include a clear session‑flow callback to the hook.
3. After each variant, note in parentheses which trigger(s) were used and how the callback was executed.

Format each variant as a continuous paragraph I can read aloud. Keep it concise—each entire ending (resolution arc + CTA) should be under 150 words.
```

**Usage:** Run this prompt after your Layer 3 script is ready. Read the three variants aloud. Use the Payoff Variant Scoring Sheet (Section 5) to pick the best.
