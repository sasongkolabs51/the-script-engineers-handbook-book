## 11. One‑Minute Pre‑Record Payoff Check

*Before you hit record, read your final 30–45 seconds aloud and answer these three questions in under a minute:*

1. **Does this ending sound like me—not like a presenter asking for a favor?**  
   ☐ Yes  ☐ No  
2. **Does it feel like the natural next beat after the emotional resolution, or does it interrupt?**  
   ☐ Natural  ☐ Interrupts  
3. **Is there a clear, satisfying callback to the opening hook, even if subtle?**  
   ☐ Yes  ☐ No  

*If any answer is “No,” pause and adjust the payoff before recording. This one minute of review can save you a retention cliff.*
