## 8. Payoff Troubleshooting Quick Reference

| **Symptom** | **Likely Cause** | **Fix** |
|-------------|------------------|---------|
| CTA still sounds robotic | You used a generic “write a CTA” prompt instead of a trigger‑specific instruction | Replace with: “Frame the subscription request as an Identity Reinforcement trigger” (or another trigger) |
| Ending feels disconnected from the rest of the script | Emotional Resolution Arc is missing or too short | Add or expand the Reflective Expansion micro‑beat; ensure the Forward Gaze explicitly links the resolution to the CTA |
| Session loop closure feels forced | Callback is too literal | Echo the hook’s theme or language in a new context, rather than repeating it verbatim |
| Voice drifts into formality at the end | Voice Profile loses salience over long scripts | Add a dedicated “Voice Check” instruction at the end of your payoff prompt: “Before outputting, verify that every sentence in the CTA matches the Voice Profile. Replace any formal constructions with my colloquial fingerprint.” |
| Viewer drops off sharply before the CTA | Emotional Resolution Arc is too long or too slow for the niche | Shorten the arc; adjust pacing tokens to match the niche’s expected tempo |
