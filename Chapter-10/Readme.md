# Chapter 10 Companion Toolkit  
*The Payoff Architect — Calls to Action, Emotional Resolution, and the Perfect Ending*

This toolkit turns the final moments of your script into a psychological engine that rewards the viewer, reinforces your brand, and drives action without ever sounding like a sales pitch. You’ll build a library of CTA triggers, construct emotional resolution arcs, design session flow callbacks that close the narrative loop, and create a repeatable Payoff Generator prompt that tailors endings to any niche and voice. By the end, your conclusions will be the strongest part of your script—not the weakest.
