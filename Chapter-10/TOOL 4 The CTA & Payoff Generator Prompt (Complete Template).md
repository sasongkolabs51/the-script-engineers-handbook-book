## TOOL 4: The CTA & Payoff Generator Prompt (Complete Template)

### *Your forever prompt. Generates three calibrated payoff variants. Paste at the end of your Layered Prompting chain.*

---

```
═══ CTA & PAYOFF GENERATOR ═══

I need the ending sequence for my script. Generate the complete
payoff: Emotional Resolution Arc + CTA. Produce THREE distinct
variants.

═══ SCRIPT CONTEXT ═══

HOOK (opening of the script — for session flow callback):
"""
[PASTE YOUR HOOK HERE]
"""

SCRIPT SUMMARY (the journey the viewer just took):
"""
[2–3 sentences summarizing the emotional arc: what problem was
explored, what insight was delivered, what transformation occurred]
"""

FRAMEWORK USED: [PAS / Hero's Journey / HSO / other]
FINAL BEAT: [the last structural beat — e.g., "Empowered Future"
or "Return with the Elixir"]

═══ NICHE & AUDIENCE ═══

NICHE: [Educational / Reaction / Story-Vlog / Review / other]
AUDIENCE: [who's watching]
AUDIENCE EXPECTATION FOR ENDINGS: [e.g., "wants to feel empowered
and know what to do next" / "wants emotional catharsis and a
gentle invitation" / "wants a clear verdict and the next comparison"]

═══ CTA TRIGGER(S) ═══

PRIMARY TRIGGER: [Identity Reinforcement / Curiosity Bridge /
Social Proof Nudge / Empowered Utility / Emotional Debt Repayment]
SECONDARY TRIGGER (optional): [___]

TRIGGER-SPECIFIC DETAILS:
- [For Identity Reinforcement: the positive identity to name]
- [For Curiosity Bridge: the specific next-video tease]
- [For Social Proof: the community behavior or proof point]
- [For Empowered Utility: the specific next gain]
- [For Emotional Debt: the "share with someone who needs this" ask]

═══ SESSION FLOW ═══

THE CALLBACK: In the Emotional Resolution Arc or CTA, directly
reference [the specific hook element: the number / the question /
the image / the promise]. Echo its language. Show it transformed
or resolved. The viewer must subconsciously register the loop
closure.

═══ VOICE & PACING ═══

VOICE: Apply my full Voice Profile. The CTA must sound EXACTLY
like the rest of the script. No tonal shift. No "presenter voice."
No formality. My voice, my rhythm, my vocabulary.

[PASTE VOICE PROFILE OR KEY ELEMENTS]

PACING FOR THE ENDING:
- Emotional Resolution Arc: [Slow down]. [Pause 1.5s] before
  the landing sentence. [Fade out energy] into the CTA.
- CTA: [your niche-appropriate pacing — e.g., "warm, moderate,
  [Pause 1s] before the specific ask"]
- Final line: [Pause 2s]. Let it breathe. Then silence.

═══ OUTPUT ═══

Generate THREE complete payoff sequences. Each includes:
1. Emotional Resolution Arc (3 micro-beats: Landing, Expansion,
   Forward Gaze) — 20–30 seconds total
2. CTA (40–60 words) using the specified trigger(s)
3. Session Flow callback integrated naturally

The three variants should differ in:
- Variant A: Primary trigger only. Straightforward.
- Variant B: Primary + secondary trigger combined. Slightly
  different emotional tone.
- Variant C: A different approach to the same triggers — maybe
  more vulnerable, more playful, or more direct.

All three must:
- Sound exactly like me (voice profile preserved)
- Include the session flow callback
- Feel continuous with the script (no tonal shift)
- Include pacing tokens
- Be 50–90 words total (resolution arc + CTA combined)

After the three variants, add:
- "Session Flow Verification": confirm the callback echoes the hook
- "Voice Check": confirm no formal constructions or banned phrases
- "Trigger Verification": name which trigger(s) each variant uses
```
