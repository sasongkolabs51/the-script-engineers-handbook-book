## 9. Payoff Integration into the Layered Prompting Chain

*Your Payoff Architecture is not an extra step; it’s an extension of your existing layers. Here’s exactly where each component fits.*

| **Layer** | **Payoff‑Related Addition** |
|-----------|----------------------------|
| **Layer 1 (Ideation)** | Choose the hook archetype and draft the session flow callback as a pair. Note the primary CTA trigger(s) for the niche. |
| **Layer 2 (Structural Skeleton)** | Expand the final framework beat into the three micro‑beats of the Emotional Resolution Arc (Landing, Expansion, Forward Gaze). |
| **Layer 3 (Pacing Injection)** | Apply niche‑specific ending pacing tokens. Include pause tokens before the CTA, and speed/emphasis tokens appropriate to the niche. |
| **Layer 4 (Voice Overlay)** | Ensure voice consistency through the payoff. Add a Voice Check instruction if your profile tends to drift at the end of long scripts. |
