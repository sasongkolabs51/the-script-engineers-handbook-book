## 2. Emotional Resolution Arc Builder Worksheet

**Purpose:** Construct the final 20–30 seconds of emotional cadence before any CTA. Use this after the last content beat (e.g., the “Empowered Future” in PAS, or “Return with the Elixir” in Hero’s Journey).

**Niche:** ________________________

### Micro‑Beat 1: Emotional Landing
*The moment when the video’s core tension is explicitly resolved. A single sentence that names the transformation the viewer has just witnessed.*

- What is the specific lesson, transformation, or payoff the video delivered?  
  ________________________________________________  
- Write one sentence that states this transformation as a closing line of a chapter (not a transition).  
  ________________________________________________

*Prompt snippet:* “Provide a single sentence that explicitly states the transformation, lesson, or payoff that the video has delivered. This sentence should feel like a closing line of a chapter, not a segue.”

### Micro‑Beat 2: Reflective Expansion
*Broadens the lens from the specific lesson to a universal truth. Connects what the viewer learned to their broader life.*

- What universal principle does this lesson point to?  
  ________________________________________________  
- Write one memorable, almost aphoristic sentence that captures this wider truth.  
  ________________________________________________

*Prompt snippet:* “Expand the core lesson into a single, universal statement that the viewer can apply beyond the specific context of the video. Use a memorable, almost aphoristic quality. Keep it brief and emotionally resonant.”

### Micro‑Beat 3: Forward Gaze
*Bridges from the resolution to the CTA. Looks ahead—either to the next video, the ongoing series, or the viewer’s own future application.*

- What will the viewer do or encounter next? (e.g., “Next time you sit down to write a proposal…” or “Next week we’re tackling…”)  
  ________________________________________________  
- Write one or two sentences that transition naturally from the reflective expansion into the chosen CTA trigger.  
  ________________________________________________

*Prompt snippet:* “Transition from the universal lesson to a forward‑looking statement. This can tease the next topic, invite reflection, or prompt a specific action, but it must feel continuous with the emotional landing, not like a new section.”

**Now compile the three beats into a single paragraph.** This is your Emotional Resolution Arc. The CTA will follow seamlessly.
