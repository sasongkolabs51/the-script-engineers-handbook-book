### The "Destination Check"

Before writing the full script, ask:

> "If the viewer ONLY watched the last 30 seconds, would they feel the weight of the journey that got them here?"

☐ Yes — the ending carries the full emotional arc.
☐ No — the ending is too generic. It could end any video. Make it specific to THIS journey.

> "Is the CTA the natural NEXT STEP after this emotional experience, or an interruption?"

☐ Natural next step — the viewer WANTS to do this.
☐ Interruption — the CTA breaks the spell. Rethink the trigger.
