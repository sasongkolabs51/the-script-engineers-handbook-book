## 7. Payoff Self‑Assessment Checklist

*After publishing a video, review your payoff’s performance using this checklist.*

☐ **Retention:** Did end‑of‑video retention hold steady compared to the middle?  
☐ **Drop‑off:** Was there a visible drop‑off in the final thirty seconds?  
☐ **Conversion:** Did subscribe‑to‑view ratios improve compared to previous videos?  
☐ **Comments:** Do comments express satisfaction, anticipation, or “can’t wait for the next one”?  
☐ **Voice:** If I read the ending aloud in isolation, does it still sound like me?  

**If any box is unchecked, identify the likely cause using the Troubleshooting Guide below and adjust your Payoff Blueprint.**
