## 8. Daily Payoff Identity Anchor

Read aloud before your payoff engineering session or whenever you’re about to write a CTA.

> The ending is not an ask; it is the final gift I give my viewer.  
> I don’t request subscriptions—I offer a next step that feels like the natural continuation of the journey we just shared.  
> I land the emotional arc, then open a door forward.  
> My hook planted a question; my payoff resolves it, leaving the viewer satisfied and eager for more.  
> I choose my triggers with care: identity, curiosity, community, utility, or shared humanity—never a generic plea.  
> My voice stays true to the last breath. The pacing guides them gently to a decision.  
> Today, my endings will not be an afterthought. They will be the part my viewers remember and act on.
